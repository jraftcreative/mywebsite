The Challenge

Great Food Doesn't Market Itself

You're running a kitchen, managing staff, and keeping customers happy — and somehow you're also expected to be a full-time content creator. Posting consistently, creating Reels, responding to reviews, and keeping up with trends is a full-time job on its own. Most restaurant owners in Singapore simply don't have the bandwidth.

The result? An Instagram feed that hasn't been updated in weeks, missed opportunities when food trends go viral, and competitors with polished content stealing your potential diners. In Singapore's food-obsessed culture, restaurants that show up consistently on social media stay top of mind — those that don't get forgotten between meals.

Social media for restaurants isn't just about pretty food photos. It's about building a community of regulars, responding to reviews that influence new customers, creating video content that the algorithm rewards, and turning online engagement into real bookings and walk-ins at your restaurant.

FAQ

Restaurant Social Media Questions Answered

How much does social media marketing cost for a restaurant in Singapore?

Our restaurant social media packages start at $2,500 SGD per month for 8 posts. The Professional package at $3,500/month includes 12 posts with Reels and stories, while the Full-Service package at $5,000+/month covers 20 posts with influencer collaborations and video production.

How many times per week should a restaurant post on social media?

We recommend a minimum of 2 feed posts per week for restaurants, supplemented with daily stories and 2–3 Reels per week. Consistency matters more than frequency — a steady posting schedule keeps your restaurant visible in feeds and builds a loyal following of food lovers.

Do you handle food photography for social media?

Yes. We coordinate professional food photography sessions and can also work with your existing photos. Our content team knows how to style, shoot, and edit food content that performs on Instagram and TikTok — from flat lays and close-ups to behind-the-scenes kitchen footage.

Can you help with influencer partnerships for my restaurant?

Yes. Our Full-Service package includes influencer collaboration management. We identify and connect with Singapore food bloggers and influencers who match your brand, negotiate partnerships, coordinate visits, and ensure content meets your quality standards before it goes live.

How do you measure social media ROI for restaurants?

We track engagement rate, follower growth, website clicks, reservation link clicks, and direct messages that convert to bookings. Monthly reports show exactly how social media drives real business results — not just likes and comments, but tangible metrics tied to diners walking through your door.
