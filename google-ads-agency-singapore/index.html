The Challenge

Wasting Ad Budget Without Clear Returns?

Too many Singapore businesses pour money into Google Ads without a proper strategy — running broad-match keywords that attract irrelevant clicks, sending traffic to pages that don't convert, and lacking the tracking needed to measure what's actually working. Every dollar wasted on unqualified clicks is a dollar that could have brought a paying customer to your door.

FAQ

Google Ads Management — Questions Answered

How much should I budget for Google Ads in Singapore?

Most Singapore businesses start with a monthly ad spend of $1,500–$5,000 on top of management fees. The right budget depends on your industry, competition level, and goals. We help you determine the optimal spend during a free strategy session and scale up as results come in.

How do you handle ad spend versus management fees?

Your ad spend goes directly to Google — we never mark it up or take a cut. Our management fee covers strategy, campaign setup, optimisation, creative, and reporting. You always have full visibility into exactly where your money is going.

How quickly will Google Ads deliver results?

Unlike SEO, Google Ads can drive traffic from day one. Most campaigns start generating clicks and leads within the first week. However, it typically takes 4–8 weeks of data and optimisation to reach peak performance and achieve the lowest cost per conversion.

Which advertising platforms do you manage?

We manage Google Search Ads, Google Display Network, YouTube Ads, Google Shopping, and remarketing campaigns. For social media advertising, we also handle Meta (Facebook and Instagram) ads. Our Growth and Scale plans cover multiple platforms.

Can I cancel my ads management plan at any time?

Yes. All our plans are month-to-month with no lock-in contracts. You can pause or cancel at any time with 30 days' notice. You retain full ownership of your Google Ads account, campaigns, and all historical data.
