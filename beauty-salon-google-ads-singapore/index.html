The Challenge

Struggling to Fill Your Appointment Book?

Singapore's beauty industry is saturated — from HDB shophouse salons to premium outlets in Orchard and Marina Bay, thousands of beauty businesses compete for the same pool of clients. When someone searches "beauty salon near me" or "facial treatment Singapore", the salons that show up first capture the bookings. If yours isn't there, potential clients are walking into your competitor's door instead.

Relying solely on walk-ins, word-of-mouth, and Instagram posts means missing the vast majority of clients who begin their search on Google. Without targeted pay-per-click campaigns, your salon remains invisible during peak search periods, your marketing spend delivers unpredictable results, and newer salons with smarter ad strategies steadily chip away at your client base.

The reality is that beauty is an impulse-driven industry in Singapore. Clients search, compare, and book — often within minutes. If you're not showing up at the exact moment they're searching, that booking goes somewhere else. Google Ads ensures you're always visible when intent is highest.

FAQ

Google Ads for Beauty Salons — Questions Answered

How much do Google Ads cost for beauty salons in Singapore?

Most beauty salons in Singapore start with a monthly ad spend of $1,000–$3,000 on top of management fees. Beauty-related keywords like "facial near me" and "beauty salon Singapore" typically cost $1.50–$6 per click. We help you set an efficient budget during a free strategy session and scale as bookings grow.

Which keywords should beauty salons target on Google Ads?

High-intent keywords include treatment-specific searches like "facial treatment Singapore", "eyelash extensions near me", and "best manicure Orchard". We also target location-based terms like "beauty salon Bugis" or "nail salon Tampines" to capture clients searching in your neighbourhood.

How quickly can Google Ads generate bookings for my beauty salon?

Beauty salons typically see their first enquiries within 48–72 hours of launching campaigns. Google Ads places you at the top of search results immediately, capturing clients who are actively looking for beauty treatments. We optimise for bookings, not just clicks, so results improve steadily each week.

Do you create landing pages for beauty salon ads?

Yes. We build conversion-optimised landing pages tailored to specific treatments or promotions — such as a facial package page or a first-visit discount offer. These dedicated pages convert ad clicks into bookings at a much higher rate than sending traffic to a generic homepage.

Can Google Ads help a new beauty salon compete with established brands?

Absolutely. Google Ads ranks you based on relevance and bid strategy, not brand history. A new beauty salon in Tanjong Pagar can appear above established chains when someone searches "facial near me" — giving you instant visibility from day one without waiting months for organic rankings.
