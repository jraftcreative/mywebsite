The Challenge

Is Your Brand Holding Your Business Back?

Too many businesses in Singapore operate without a defined brand identity. The logo was made on Canva. The colours change depending on who creates the next post. There are no brand guidelines, so every piece of marketing looks different from the last. The result is a brand that feels inconsistent, forgettable, and unprofessional.

Without a cohesive visual identity, your business struggles to stand out in a crowded market. Customers can not recognise you across channels. Your marketing materials clash with each other. And every time you hire a new designer or agency, they start from scratch because there is no system to follow.

Professional branding solves this by giving your business a clear visual foundation — a logo that works everywhere, a defined colour palette, consistent typography, and a brand guidelines document that keeps everything aligned. It is the difference between looking like a side project and looking like an established business.

FAQ

Branding Questions Answered

How much does branding cost in Singapore?

Professional branding in Singapore typically ranges from $3,500 to $15,000+ depending on the scope. A basic logo and brand guidelines package starts at $3,500 SGD, while a full brand identity system with strategy and collateral design starts from $7,500 SGD. Our Premium package covers end-to-end branding including strategy, positioning, and rollout support.

What is included in a brand identity package?

A brand identity package typically includes a custom logo (with variations for different formats), a defined colour palette, typography selections, a brand guidelines document, and usage rules for consistent application across all touchpoints. More comprehensive packages add brand strategy, messaging frameworks, business card and stationery design, social media templates, and presentation decks.

How long does the branding process take?

A typical branding project takes 3 to 6 weeks from discovery to final delivery. Simpler packages with logo and basic guidelines can be completed in 2 to 3 weeks. More comprehensive projects involving brand strategy, multiple collateral items, and stakeholder reviews may take 6 to 8 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during the discovery phase.

Do I need branding before I build my website?

Ideally, yes. Your brand identity — logo, colours, typography, and visual style — directly informs your website design. Starting with branding ensures your website, social media, and all marketing materials are visually consistent from day one. However, we can also run branding and web design in parallel to save time.

Can you rebrand an existing business?

Absolutely. We work with established businesses that have outgrown their original branding or need to reposition in the market. A rebrand can include a refreshed logo, updated brand guidelines, a new visual identity, and a rollout plan to update all existing touchpoints — from your website to business cards and social media profiles.
