The Challenge

Clients Searching "Spa Near Me" Won't Find You Without a Website

"Spa near me", "massage Singapore", and "facial treatment near Orchard" are searched thousands of times every month by people ready to book. These are high-intent clients looking for a spa right now — and they're choosing from the results Google shows them. If your spa doesn't have a professional website, you're invisible to this audience and handing bookings to competitors who invested in their online presence.

A spa website does what your Instagram page and marketplace listing cannot. It displays your full treatment menu with descriptions, durations, and pricing. It lets clients book appointments directly — even at midnight after a stressful day when they need a massage most. It showcases your spa's ambience through immersive imagery so first-time visitors feel comfortable before they arrive. And it sells gift vouchers around the clock, turning your website into a revenue stream that never sleeps.

Singapore's spa and wellness market is growing rapidly, with new outlets opening in malls, shophouses, and mixed-use developments across the island. Whether you operate a luxury day spa in Marina Bay, a traditional Thai massage studio in Jalan Besar, or a boutique facial spa in Holland Village, a well-designed website is what separates you from the hundreds of spas competing for the same clients.

What We Build for Spas

Treatment Menu & Packages

Organised treatment pages with descriptions, durations, pricing, and package bundles. Categorised by type — massage, facial, body scrub, wellness rituals — so clients can browse, compare, and choose the perfect treatment.

Online Booking Integration

Seamless integration with Fresha, Booksy, or your existing booking platform. Clients select a treatment, choose a therapist, pick a date and time, and confirm — no phone calls, no WhatsApp back-and-forth, no missed bookings.

Ambience Showcase

Immersive photography galleries, ambient colour palettes, and optional video backgrounds that convey the tranquillity of your space. Premium packages include 360-degree virtual tours so clients experience your spa environment before booking.

FAQ

Spa Website Questions Answered

How much does a spa website cost in Singapore?

A professional spa website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with your treatment menu, ambience gallery, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add online booking, gift vouchers, and wellness package pages.

Can you integrate online booking into my spa website?

Yes. We integrate with Fresha, Booksy, and other booking platforms used by spas in Singapore. Clients can browse treatments, select a therapist, choose a time slot, and confirm their booking directly on your website — reducing no-shows and phone enquiries.

Do you build gift voucher systems for spa websites?

Absolutely. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include a digital gift voucher system. Clients can purchase spa vouchers for specific treatments or monetary values directly online — a proven revenue driver especially during festive seasons and Mother's Day in Singapore.

Can you showcase the spa ambience on my website?

Yes. We use high-quality imagery, ambient colour palettes, and optional video backgrounds to convey your spa's atmosphere online. Premium packages can include a 360-degree virtual tour so potential clients experience your space before stepping through the door.

How long does it take to build a spa website?

Most spa websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A Starter site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with booking integration, gift vouchers, or virtual tours typically take 3–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
