SEO Services for Restaurants in Singapore

Every day, thousands of people in Singapore search Google for where to eat. If your restaurant doesn't rank on page one, those diners are walking into your competitor's door instead. We build SEO strategies that put your restaurant at the top of local search.

The Challenge

Thousands of Restaurants — Can Diners Find Yours?

Singapore has over 7,000 restaurants competing for the same pool of diners. When someone searches "best Italian restaurant Tanjong Pagar" or "dinner near me" on their phone, the top three results on Google Maps capture the overwhelming majority of walk-in traffic. If your restaurant isn't ranking, you're invisible to hungry customers ready to spend.

Relying solely on food delivery platforms means giving up 30% of every order in commissions. Organic search is the most cost-effective channel for driving direct reservations, dine-in traffic, and takeaway orders — but only if your website is properly optimised for the terms your potential customers actually search.

Most restaurant websites are thin — a homepage, an "About" page, and a PDF menu. Without dedicated pages for your cuisine, location, and services, without schema markup that tells Google you're a restaurant, and without a properly managed Google Business Profile, even the best food in the neighbourhood stays undiscovered online.

Our SEO Services

SEO Built for Restaurants

Restaurant SEO is a specialist discipline. Diners search by cuisine type, neighbourhood, occasion, and price range — creating dozens of keyword combinations that need dedicated pages and strong local signals. We build SEO strategies specifically for how Singapore diners research and choose where to eat.

Pair SEO with a professionally designed restaurant website, social media marketing for food content that drives engagement, or Google Ads to capture high-intent diners during peak periods.

Technical SEO Audit

Complete crawl of your restaurant website to find indexing issues, broken links, missing structured data, slow load times, and mobile usability problems that stop Google from ranking your restaurant for local searches.

Menu & Page Optimisation

Keyword-optimised pages for your cuisine, location, and dining experiences. Proper meta titles, descriptions, heading structures, and Restaurant schema markup so Google understands exactly what you serve and where.

Local SEO & Google Maps

Google Business Profile optimisation with correct restaurant categories, food photos, menu uploads, review management, and local citation building to rank in the Maps 3-pack for "restaurant near me" searches.

Review & Reputation Management

Systematic review generation from satisfied diners, professional response templates, and monitoring across Google, TripAdvisor, and other platforms. More positive reviews mean higher local rankings and more bookings.

FAQ

Restaurant SEO Questions Answered

How much does SEO cost for a restaurant in Singapore?

Restaurant SEO in Singapore ranges from $800 to $3,000+ per month. Our Essential plan at $800 SGD/month covers technical SEO, on-page optimisation, and Google Business Profile setup. Growth and Enterprise plans add content strategy, review management, and multi-location SEO for restaurant groups.

How long does it take for restaurant SEO to show results?

Most restaurants see measurable ranking improvements within 3 to 4 months. Google Business Profile optimisation can produce quick wins in weeks. Location-specific keywords like "Italian restaurant Tanjong Pagar" or "best ramen Bugis" rank faster than broad competitive terms like "restaurant Singapore".

Can SEO help my restaurant rank on Google Maps?

Yes. Google Maps ranking is central to our restaurant SEO service. We optimise your Google Business Profile with correct categories, professional food photos, menu uploads, review management, and local citation building to get your restaurant into the Maps 3-pack for "restaurant near me" searches.

Do you help with review management for restaurants?

Yes. Google reviews directly impact your local search ranking. Our Growth plan and above include review generation strategy, response templates, and monitoring. We help you systematically collect genuine reviews from satisfied diners and respond professionally to all feedback.

Can you do SEO for multiple restaurant locations?

Absolutely. Our Enterprise plan specialises in multi-location restaurant SEO. We create unique, optimised pages for each outlet, manage individual Google Business Profiles, and build location-specific citations so each branch ranks independently in its own neighbourhood.
