The Challenge

Parents Are Searching — Can They Find Your Art School?

Every week, thousands of parents in Singapore search "art class for kids near me", "drawing lessons Singapore", or "pottery class for children" on Google. They compare enrichment centres side by side, scrolling through student artwork, reading instructor bios, and checking class schedules by age group. If your art school doesn't have a professional website, you're invisible to these families — and they're booking trial classes with your competitors instead.

A Facebook page or an Instagram account alone is not enough. Parents researching art enrichment for their children want to see the full range of programmes you offer — drawing, painting, pottery, digital art — understand each instructor's qualifications and teaching approach, view galleries of student work at different skill levels, and check available class timings — all before they ever call or walk in. They want proof that your school produces real results, not just a social media feed.

Art schools that invest in a proper website with clear programme information, compelling student galleries, and easy trial class booking consistently outperform those relying on word of mouth or marketplace listings alone. In a market as competitive as Singapore's art enrichment scene — from established chains to boutique studios in Bukit Timah, Tanjong Pagar, and Katong — your website is the first impression that determines whether a parent chooses your school or scrolls past.

What We Build

What We Build for Art Schools

Every art school website we deliver is built to convert visitors into enrolled students. We go beyond a generic template — your site will feature dedicated course pages for drawing, painting, pottery, and digital art, student gallery showcases that demonstrate your teaching quality, and a frictionless booking flow that makes it easy to sign up for a trial class.

A great website is just the starting point. Pair your art school site with local SEO services to rank for "art class near me" searches in your neighbourhood, social media marketing to reach parents on Instagram and Facebook, and Google Ads to capture high-intent searches during peak enrolment periods like June and December holidays.

Course & Programme Pages

Dedicated pages for every medium and programme you offer — drawing, painting, watercolour, pottery, sculpture, and digital art. Each page details class formats, age groups from toddler to adult, skill levels from beginner to portfolio prep, and term schedules so parents find exactly the right fit for their child.

Trial Class Booking

Let parents book trial classes directly through your website. The booking system displays class availability by day and time, filters by age group and medium, and sends automated confirmation emails — reducing phone calls and giving your team more time to focus on students in the studio.

Student Gallery & Showcases

A filterable gallery that lets visitors browse student artwork by medium, age group, or programme. Lightbox viewing, high-resolution images, and the option to feature standout pieces on the homepage — proof that your school produces real creative growth parents can see.

Instructor Portfolios & Exhibition Pages

Each instructor gets a profile page with their artistic background, qualifications, teaching philosophy, and a mini portfolio of their own work. Pair these with exhibition and event pages — open houses, student showcases, holiday workshops — to keep your community engaged year-round.

FAQ

Art School Website Questions Answered

How much does an art school website cost in Singapore?

A professional art school website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on features and complexity. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page website with course information, a student gallery, contact form, Google Maps integration, and basic SEO. Business and Premium packages add trial class booking, instructor portfolios, age-group programmes, exhibition event pages, online registration, and payment integration.

Can you build a student gallery to showcase artwork on our website?

Yes. Every package includes a student gallery section, but our Business and Premium tiers take it further with filterable galleries organised by medium — drawing, painting, pottery, digital art — age group, or programme. Students and parents can browse artwork in a lightbox view, and you can update the gallery easily as new pieces are completed. It's one of the most powerful ways to demonstrate the quality of your teaching to prospective families.

Can parents register for classes and make payments online?

Yes. Our Business and Premium packages include online registration forms that let parents sign up for trial classes, workshops, or term-long programmes directly through your website. The Premium tier adds payment integration so parents can pay course fees online via credit card or PayNow — reducing admin work and making enrolment seamless for busy Singapore families.

Will you optimise our art school website for local search in Singapore?

Yes. We optimise your website for keywords parents actually search — like "art class for kids Singapore", "drawing lessons near me", or "pottery class Bukit Timah". This includes on-page SEO, schema markup for art schools, Google Business Profile optimisation, and location-targeted content that helps you rank in local search results and Google Maps for your specific neighbourhood.

How long does it take to build an art school website?

Most art school websites go live within 2 weeks for our Starter package. Business-tier sites with trial class booking, instructor portfolios, and exhibition pages typically take 3 weeks, while Premium builds with online registration, payment integration, and multi-location support take 4-5 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting so you can plan around your term schedule.
