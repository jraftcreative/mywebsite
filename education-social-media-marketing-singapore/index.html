The Challenge

Singapore's Education Market Is Crowded — Standing Out Takes Strategy

Singapore spends more per capita on education than almost any country in the world. From tuition centres and enrichment programmes to preschools, international schools, and corporate training providers, the education sector is vast, competitive, and constantly evolving. Every segment is fighting for attention from the same pool of parents, learners, and decision-makers — and the ones who win are the ones who show up consistently where their audience is already spending time: social media.

Yet most education businesses in Singapore still treat social media as an afterthought. A Facebook page that hasn't been updated since the last open house. An Instagram account with a handful of stock photos and no cohesive brand. A TikTok presence that doesn't exist. Meanwhile, competitors with polished feeds, regular Reels, and active community engagement are building the trust and visibility that translate directly into enrolments. In a market where parents and learners compare options side by side on their phones, a weak social media presence is a revenue leak you can't afford.

Education Social Media Marketing — Questions Answered

How much does social media marketing cost for education businesses in Singapore?

Social media marketing for education businesses in Singapore ranges from $2,500 to $5,000+ per month. Our Essential plan starts at $2,500 SGD/month for 8 posts across 2 platforms, with Professional and Full-Service plans offering more content, video production, community management, and paid social integration.

What types of education businesses do you work with?

We work with all segments of the education industry in Singapore — tuition centres, enrichment centres, preschools, childcare centres, international schools, private education institutions, training providers, and edtech companies. Each segment gets a tailored content strategy based on its target audience and enrolment cycle.

Which social media platforms are best for education marketing in Singapore?

The best platforms depend on your audience. Facebook and Instagram are essential for parent-focused education businesses like preschools and tuition centres. LinkedIn works well for corporate training providers and higher education. TikTok is increasingly effective for reaching younger learners and millennial parents. We recommend the right platform mix based on your specific segment.

Do you offer paid social media advertising for education businesses?

Yes. Our Full-Service plan includes paid social integration, and we can add targeted advertising to any plan. We run Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns for lead generation, enrolment drives, and open house promotions — all optimised for Singapore's education market with detailed performance reporting.

How do you create content for different education segments?

We tailor content to each segment's audience and goals. Tuition centres get exam tips and student success content. Preschools and childcare centres get warm, trust-building daily activity posts. Enrichment centres get class highlight Reels and programme launches. Training providers get thought leadership and course promotion content. Every piece is written for the specific decision-maker in your market.
