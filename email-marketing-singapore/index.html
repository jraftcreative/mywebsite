The Challenge

Low Open Rates & Emails Landing in Spam?

Most Singapore businesses struggle with email marketing that underperforms — low open rates, minimal click-throughs, and campaigns that end up in spam folders instead of inboxes. Without proper segmentation, every subscriber gets the same generic message regardless of their interests or where they are in the buying journey.

On top of that, PDPA compliance adds another layer of complexity. Sending emails without proper consent mechanisms, missing unsubscribe links, or failing to maintain auditable opt-in records can result in fines of up to $1 million. Meanwhile, your competitors with well-segmented lists and automated drip sequences are converting subscribers into paying customers on autopilot.

FAQ

Email Marketing — Questions Answered

Which email marketing platforms do you work with?

We work with all major platforms including Mailchimp, Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign, HubSpot, and Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). We recommend the best platform based on your business size, budget, and automation needs. If you already use a platform, we can work within your existing setup.

How do you ensure emails are PDPA compliant in Singapore?

Every campaign we send follows Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act. We implement proper consent collection mechanisms, maintain Do Not Call registry compliance, include mandatory unsubscribe links, and keep auditable opt-in records. We also advise on data retention policies and help you build compliant sign-up forms.

What kind of open rates can I expect?

Average open rates depend on your industry, but most of our clients see rates between 25% and 45% — well above the global average of 21%. We achieve this through proper list hygiene, segmentation, personalised subject lines, and send-time optimisation tailored to Singapore audiences.

How often should I send marketing emails?

Frequency depends on your audience and content type. Most Singapore businesses see the best engagement with 1–2 emails per week. We monitor engagement metrics closely and adjust frequency to avoid subscriber fatigue while keeping your brand top of mind.

Can you help grow my email subscriber list?

Yes. We design opt-in forms, landing pages, lead magnets, and pop-ups that convert website visitors into subscribers. Combined with proper segmentation from day one, this ensures your list grows with engaged contacts who are genuinely interested in your offerings.
