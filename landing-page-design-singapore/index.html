The Challenge

Your Ad Spend Deserves Better Than a Generic Page

You're investing in Google Ads, Meta campaigns, or email marketing — but sending that traffic to your homepage or a generic services page. The result: high bounce rates, low conversion rates, and ad spend that delivers a fraction of what it should. Visitors land, get distracted by navigation options, and leave without taking action.

Without a purpose-built landing page, there's no clear call to action, no message match between your ad and the page, and no way to A/B test what actually drives conversions. You're guessing instead of optimising. Meanwhile, competitors with dedicated landing pages are converting the same traffic at two to three times your rate.

Every campaign needs a page that's built for one job: turning visitors into leads or customers. That means focused messaging, a single conversion goal, and data-driven design — not a one-size-fits-all page pulled from a template library.

FAQ

Landing Page Design — Questions Answered

How much does a landing page cost in Singapore?

A professionally designed landing page in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on complexity and scope. Our Single Page package starts at $2,500 SGD and includes a conversion-focused design, lead capture form, and mobile-responsive layout. Campaign and Full Funnel packages add A/B testing, multiple pages, and ongoing optimisation.

What is the difference between a landing page and a website?

A website is a multi-page site that serves many purposes — company information, blog, services, contact details. A landing page is a single, focused page designed for one specific goal: getting a visitor to take action. Landing pages strip away navigation distractions and guide every visitor toward a single conversion — filling out a form, booking a call, or making a purchase.

How do you measure landing page performance?

We track conversion rate, bounce rate, time on page, scroll depth, and form completion rate. We also set up heatmap tracking to see exactly where visitors click and where they drop off. Every landing page we build includes Google Analytics and conversion tracking from day one so you can measure ROI from the first visitor.

Do you integrate landing pages with Google Ads and Meta?

Yes. We build landing pages specifically designed to work with your ad campaigns. We install Google Ads conversion tracking, Meta Pixel, and any other tracking pixels you need. We also align the landing page messaging with your ad copy to maintain consistency and improve Quality Score, which lowers your cost per click.

How long does it take to design a landing page?

A single landing page is typically ready within 5–7 business days from content approval. Campaign packages with multiple pages and A/B variants take 2–3 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting and keep you updated at every stage so your landing page is ready in time for your campaign launch.
