The Challenge

Word-of-Mouth Alone Won't Fill Your Classrooms

Singapore has over 800 registered tuition centres, plus thousands of freelance tutors competing for the same students. Parents today research online before making enrolment decisions — comparing Google results, reading reviews, checking social media, and visiting websites. If your centre isn't visible across these channels, you're losing enrolments to competitors who are.

Many tuition centres rely heavily on referrals and flyer distribution. While referrals remain valuable, they don't scale predictably. A parent searching "PSLE maths tuition Tampines" on Google won't find your centre through word-of-mouth — they'll find whichever competitor ranks on page one. Meanwhile, centres with no social media presence miss the opportunity to build trust with parents who check Instagram and Facebook before booking a trial lesson.

Full-service marketing solves this by building visibility across every channel parents use — a professional website that converts, SEO that captures search traffic, ads that drive immediate enquiries, and social content that builds credibility. One coordinated strategy instead of scattered efforts.

FAQ

Tuition Centre Marketing — Questions Answered

How much should a tuition centre spend on marketing in Singapore?

Most tuition centres in Singapore invest between $2,500 and $8,000 per month on marketing. A typical allocation covers website maintenance, SEO, Google Ads, and social media content. The right budget depends on your centre's size, number of branches, and enrolment targets. Our Essential plan at $3,000/month is a strong starting point for single-branch centres looking to build a solid marketing foundation.

How long before marketing generates enrolments for my tuition centre?

Paid channels like Google Ads can generate parent enquiries within the first week. SEO typically takes 3–6 months to build sustainable organic traffic. Social media builds brand awareness over 2–3 months. Most clients see a meaningful increase in trial lesson bookings within the first 60 days of a full-service campaign.

What marketing channels work best for tuition centres in Singapore?

Google Search — both organic and ads — is the highest-intent channel because parents actively searching for tuition are ready to enrol. Facebook and Instagram work well for awareness and retargeting. WhatsApp is increasingly important for parent communication. A well-designed website ties everything together as your conversion hub.

Do you handle all marketing or just specific services?

We are a full-service agency — website design, SEO, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, social media content, and email marketing all handled by one team. You can start with specific services and expand over time, or go all-in with an integrated strategy from day one. Every channel is managed in-house with no outsourcing.

Can you help during peak enrolment seasons like year-end and January?

Yes. We plan seasonal campaigns around Singapore's key enrolment periods — year-end registration, January intake, mid-year transfers, and exam result release dates. This includes ramping up ad budgets, launching targeted landing pages, scheduling open house promotions, and running lead nurturing sequences timed to parent decision cycles.
