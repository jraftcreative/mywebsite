FAQ

Facebook Ads for Tuition Centres — Questions Answered

How much do Facebook Ads cost for tuition centres in Singapore?

Most tuition centres start with a monthly ad spend of $1,000–$3,500 on top of management fees. Facebook and Instagram ads targeting Singapore parents typically cost $0.50–$3 per click, which is often lower than Google Ads. We help you determine the right budget during a free strategy session based on your subject offerings and target areas.

How do you target parents of school-age children on Facebook?

We use Meta's detailed targeting to reach parents with children of specific age groups — primary school, secondary school, or JC level. Combined with location targeting around your centre, interest-based filters (education, parenting, academic achievement), and lookalike audiences from your existing student database, we ensure your ads reach parents most likely to enrol.

What types of Facebook ad creative work best for tuition centres?

Parent testimonials and student success stories consistently deliver the highest engagement and lowest cost per lead. We also run carousel ads showcasing different subjects and programmes, video ads featuring your teaching environment, and lead form ads that let parents enquire directly without leaving Facebook. All creative is A/B tested to identify top performers.

Should my tuition centre use Facebook Ads or Google Ads?

Both serve different purposes. Google Ads captures parents actively searching for tuition — high intent, ready to enrol. Facebook Ads reaches parents who fit your ideal profile but may not be actively searching — building awareness and capturing demand before they go to Google. For maximum enrolments, we recommend running both platforms together.

Can Facebook lead forms really generate tuition centre enquiries?

Yes. Meta lead forms are particularly effective for tuition centres because parents can submit their child's level, subject needs, and contact details without leaving Facebook. This reduces friction significantly. We set up instant follow-up notifications so you can contact the parent within minutes — which dramatically improves conversion rates.
