The Challenge

Clients Search Google Before Booking a Haircut — Is Your Salon Showing Up?

Every day, hundreds of people in Singapore open Google and search for "hair salon near me", "balayage Singapore", or "Korean perm Orchard". They're not browsing for fun — they have an appointment to book. But most hair salons don't appear in these results because they've never invested in SEO. They rely on Instagram, referrals, and walk-ins, missing the largest source of new clients: people actively searching for a hair salon right now.

The hair salons that dominate Google's first page didn't get there by accident. They have optimised Google Business Profiles with hundreds of reviews, dedicated pages for every service they offer — balayage, perms, keratin treatments, hair colouring — and blog content that answers the questions people search before booking. Without this SEO foundation, your salon remains invisible to the thousands of potential clients who search for hair services online every month.

Singapore's hair salon market is dense. Korean-style salons cluster around Somerset and Tanjong Pagar, Japanese salons dominate Liang Court and Robertson Quay, and neighbourhood unisex salons are on every HDB estate. Standing out requires more than good stylists — it requires being the salon that Google recommends when someone in your area searches for exactly the service you offer.

Our SEO Services

SEO Built for Hair Salons

Hair salon SEO isn't generic marketing. Clients search by service, style, neighbourhood, and even specific stylist types — creating hundreds of keyword opportunities. We build SEO strategies designed around how people in Singapore actually search for and choose hair salons.

Pair SEO with a professionally designed hair salon website, social media marketing for stylist portfolio content, or Google Ads to capture high-intent searches during peak booking periods.

Technical SEO Audit

Complete crawl of your hair salon website to identify indexing issues, broken links, missing schema markup, slow load times, and mobile usability problems. We fix every technical barrier preventing Google from ranking your salon for "hair salon near me" and service-specific searches.

Stylist Service Keywords

Keyword-optimised pages for every service your salon offers — balayage, Korean perms, Japanese straightening, hair colouring, keratin treatments, scalp treatments. Each page targets the exact phrases clients type into Google, like "balayage near Orchard" or "Korean perm Tanjong Pagar".

Neighbourhood Targeting

Hair salons are hyper-local businesses. We optimise your Google Business Profile, build neighbourhood-specific citations, and create location-targeted content so your salon ranks in the Google Maps 3-pack for every "hair salon near me" search within your catchment area.

Review Generation Strategy

A structured system for generating authentic Google reviews from satisfied clients. We set up automated post-appointment review requests, train your staff on when and how to ask, and help you respond to every review — building the social proof that drives both rankings and client confidence.
