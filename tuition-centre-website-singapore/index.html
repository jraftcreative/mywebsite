The Challenge

Parents Are Searching — Can They Find You?

Every week, thousands of parents in Singapore search "tuition near me" on Google. They compare centres side by side, reading reviews, checking class schedules, and looking at teacher credentials. If your tuition centre doesn't have a professional website, you're invisible to these parents — and they're enrolling their children with your competitors instead.

A Facebook page or a listing on a tuition directory is not enough. Parents want to see your full programme lineup, understand your teaching methodology, and read real testimonials from other parents. They want to know your class sizes, your tutors' qualifications, and whether you offer trial classes — all before they ever pick up the phone.

Tuition centres that invest in a proper website with clear class information, visible results, and easy enrolment consistently outperform those relying on word of mouth alone. In a market as competitive as Singapore's education sector, your website is the first impression that determines whether a parent chooses you or scrolls past.

What We Build

What We Build for Tuition Centres

Every tuition centre website we deliver is built to convert visitors into enrolled students. We go beyond a generic school template — your site will feature structured class schedules, compelling teacher profiles, and a frictionless enrolment flow that makes it easy for parents to sign up.

A great website is just the starting point. Pair your tuition centre site with local SEO services to rank for "tuition near me" searches in your area, social media marketing to reach parents on Facebook and Instagram, and Google Ads to capture high-intent searches during peak enrolment periods.

Class Schedules & Timetables

Organised, easy-to-read timetables for every subject and level. Parents can quickly find available slots, check timings, and see which branch offers the classes they need — without calling to ask.

Online Enrolment Forms

Let parents register their children directly through your website. Capture student details, subject preferences, and preferred timings in one streamlined form — reducing phone tag and manual admin work.

Parent Testimonials & Results

Showcase real testimonials from satisfied parents and highlight student results. Visible proof of your track record builds trust and gives hesitant parents the confidence to enrol their children.

Subject & Programme Pages

Dedicated pages for each subject and level you offer — from primary school Maths to O-Level Science. Detailed programme descriptions help parents understand your curriculum and teaching approach.

FAQ

Tuition Centre Website Questions Answered

How much does a tuition centre website cost in Singapore?

A professional tuition centre website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on features and complexity. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page website with class information, a contact form, Google Maps integration, and basic SEO. Business and Premium packages add online enrolment, teacher profiles, parent portals, and multi-branch support.

Can you integrate online enrolment forms into our website?

Yes. Our Business and Premium packages include online enrolment forms that let parents register their children directly through your website. Forms can capture student details, preferred class timings, subject selections, and even process deposit payments — reducing your admin workload and making enrolment seamless for parents.

Do you support multi-branch tuition centres?

Yes. Our Premium package is designed for tuition centres with multiple branches across Singapore. Each branch gets its own page with location-specific class schedules, teacher profiles, Google Maps embed, and unique local SEO targeting so parents searching near each branch can find you.

How do you help tuition centres rank on Google?

We optimise your website for keywords parents actually search — like "tuition near me", "maths tuition Tampines", or "primary school English tuition Singapore". This includes on-page SEO, schema markup for educational organisations, Google Business Profile optimisation, and location-targeted content that helps each branch rank in local search results.

How long does it take to build a tuition centre website?

Most tuition centre websites go live within 2 weeks for our Starter package. Business-tier sites with online enrolment and blog typically take 3 weeks, while Premium builds with multi-branch support and parent portals take 4-5 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting so you can plan around your term schedule.
