The Challenge

Missing the TikTok Opportunity While Your Competitors Move In?

TikTok is not just another social media platform — it is the fastest-growing discovery engine in Southeast Asia. Singapore consumers use TikTok to find restaurants, compare products, research services, and make purchase decisions. Brands that are not on TikTok are invisible to an audience that spends more time there than on any other app.

Repurposing Instagram Reels or Facebook content and posting it to TikTok does not work. The algorithm rewards native content built around trending sounds, platform-specific editing styles, and authentic storytelling. Without a TikTok-first strategy, your content gets buried, your ad budget underperforms, and your brand misses a channel that your competitors are already using to win customers.

TikTok Marketing — Questions Answered

How much does TikTok marketing cost in Singapore?

TikTok marketing in Singapore typically costs between $1,500 and $5,000+ per month depending on content volume, ad spend management, and influencer partnerships. Our Starter plan begins at $1,500 SGD/month for 8 videos and strategy, while our Growth and Scale plans add TikTok Ads management, creator collaborations, and TikTok Shop integration.

Can I repurpose my Instagram content for TikTok?

While some content concepts can translate, directly reposting Instagram Reels to TikTok rarely performs well. TikTok's algorithm rewards native content that follows platform-specific trends, sounds, and editing styles. We create TikTok-first content designed around the platform's culture and discovery mechanics to maximise reach and engagement.

How long does it take to grow a TikTok account in Singapore?

TikTok's algorithm can surface content to large audiences quickly, so individual videos can gain traction within days. Building a consistent, engaged following typically takes 2-3 months of regular posting with a clear content strategy. We focus on content pillars, trending formats, and audience engagement to accelerate growth from the start.

Do you manage TikTok Ads as well as organic content?

Yes. Our Growth and Scale plans include TikTok Ads management through TikTok Ads Manager.
