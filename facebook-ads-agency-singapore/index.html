FAQ

Facebook Ads Management — Questions Answered

How much should I spend on Facebook Ads in Singapore?

Most Singapore businesses start with a monthly ad spend of $1,000-$5,000 on top of management fees. The right budget depends on your industry, audience size, and campaign objectives. We help you determine the optimal spend during a free strategy session and scale up once we identify what works.

What is the difference between boosting a post and running Facebook Ads?

Boosting a post is a simplified ad option with limited targeting and optimisation. A proper Facebook Ads campaign built through Meta Ads Manager gives you full control over audience targeting, ad placements, bidding strategies, conversion tracking, and A/B testing. The difference in ROI is significant — boosted posts rarely deliver the same cost-per-result as a strategically managed campaign.

Do Facebook Ads work for B2B businesses in Singapore?

Yes. Facebook and Instagram have strong B2B targeting capabilities through job title, industry, company size, and interest-based audiences. Combined with retargeting and lookalike audiences built from your existing customer data, Meta ads can be highly effective for B2B lead generation in Singapore.

How long does it take to see results from Facebook Ads?

You can start seeing impressions, clicks, and leads within the first few days. However, it typically takes 2-4 weeks for Meta's algorithm to complete its learning phase and optimise delivery. Most campaigns reach stable, cost-efficient performance after 4-6 weeks of data and ongoing optimisation.

Do you manage Instagram Ads as well?

Yes. Facebook and Instagram advertising are both managed through Meta Ads Manager, so our campaigns cover both platforms by default. We create platform-specific ad creatives and optimise placements across Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Stories, Reels, and the Audience Network to maximise your reach and results.
