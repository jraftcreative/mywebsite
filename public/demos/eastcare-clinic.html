Our Services

Comprehensive Care for Your Family

From routine check-ups to chronic disease management, we provide holistic healthcare tailored to your needs.

GP Consultation

General health consultations for cough, cold, fever, allergies, skin conditions, and more. From $20 (before CHAS subsidy).

Health Screening

Comprehensive health screening packages including blood tests, cholesterol, diabetes, and cancer markers. Screen for Life eligible.

Vaccinations

Flu, pneumococcal, HPV, Hepatitis B, and childhood immunisations. MediSave claimable for selected vaccinations.

Chronic Disease Management

Long-term care for diabetes, hypertension, high cholesterol, and asthma. CDMP subsidies available for eligible patients.

Minor Procedures

Wound stitching, abscess drainage, mole and skin tag removal, ear syringing, and ingrown toenail treatment.

Medical Certificates

MC for work or school, pre-employment check-ups, fitness assessments, and referral letters to specialist clinics.
