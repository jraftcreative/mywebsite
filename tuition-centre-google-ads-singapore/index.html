The Challenge

Struggling to Fill Seats at Your Tuition Centre?

Singapore's tuition industry is one of the most competitive in the world — parents spend over S$1.4 billion annually on private tuition, and thousands of centres compete for every enrolment. If your tuition centre doesn't appear when parents search "PSLE tuition near me" or "Math tuition Singapore", your competitors are capturing those students instead.

Relying on word-of-mouth and flyers alone means missing the 80% of parents who start their tuition search on Google. Without targeted ads, you're invisible during the critical enrolment windows in January and July, your cost per enquiry is unpredictable, and larger centres with bigger marketing budgets dominate the search results that matter most.

FAQ

Google Ads for Tuition Centres — Questions Answered

How much do Google Ads cost for tuition centres in Singapore?

Most tuition centres start with a monthly ad spend of $1,500–$4,000 on top of management fees. Education-related keywords like "PSLE tuition" and "Math tuition Singapore" typically cost $2–$8 per click. We help you set the right budget during a free strategy session and scale once enrolments come in.

Which keywords should tuition centres target on Google Ads?

High-intent keywords include subject-specific searches like "PSLE Math tuition Singapore", "Secondary Science tuition near me", and "O Level Chemistry tuition". We also target location-based terms like "tuition centre Bishan" or "English tuition Tampines" to capture parents searching in your area.

When is the best time to run Google Ads for a tuition centre?

Enrolment peaks in January (new academic year) and July (mid-year intake). We recommend increasing budgets 4–6 weeks before these peaks to capture early searchers. That said, parents search for tuition year-round — especially after exam results are released — so maintaining a baseline campaign ensures steady enrolments throughout the year.

How do you track enrolments from Google Ads?

We set up conversion tracking on your website to monitor form submissions, phone calls, and WhatsApp clicks from ad traffic. Each enquiry is attributed to the specific keyword and ad that drove it, so you know exactly which campaigns are generating trial lesson sign-ups and enrolments.

Can Google Ads work for a new tuition centre with no brand recognition?

Absolutely. Google Ads levels the playing field because you appear at the top of search results based on relevance and bid strategy, not brand awareness. New tuition centres can start generating enquiries from day one by targeting parents actively searching for tuition — even before building an organic presence.
