FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I spend on ads?

For Singapore SMEs, we recommend starting with S$1,000–S$3,000 per month in ad spend (separate from management fees). This allows enough data to optimise campaigns effectively. We help you set the right budget based on your goals and industry.

Which ad platform is best for my business?

Google Ads works best for capturing high-intent search traffic. Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram) are ideal for brand awareness and visual products. TikTok suits younger audiences. We recommend the best mix during our free strategy session.

How quickly will I see results from paid ads?

Unlike SEO, paid ads can drive traffic immediately. However, campaigns typically need 2–4 weeks of data collection and optimisation before reaching peak performance. Most clients see meaningful results within the first month.

Do management fees include the ad spend?

No. Management fees cover strategy, campaign setup, creative production, optimisation, and reporting. Ad spend is paid directly to the platforms (Google, Meta, TikTok) and is a separate budget that you control.

Can I pause or cancel my campaign?

Yes. We have no lock-in contracts. You can pause or cancel campaigns at any time. We recommend running campaigns for at least 3 months to gather enough data for meaningful optimisation and results.
