The Challenge

Your Website Should Be Your Best Salesperson

Most businesses in Singapore lose customers to these common website problems.

Outdated Design

A dated website signals an outdated business. Visitors judge your credibility within 0.05 seconds — and leave if it looks unprofessional.

Not Mobile-Friendly

Over 72% of Singapore web traffic is mobile. If your site isn't responsive, you're invisible to most of your potential customers.

Painfully Slow

53% of visitors leave if a page takes longer than 3 seconds to load. Slow sites kill conversions and hurt your Google rankings.

No Clear CTA

Visitors land on your site but don't know what to do next. Without clear calls-to-action, traffic doesn't convert into enquiries.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

A typical business website takes 4–6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Ecommerce stores and web applications may take 8–12 weeks depending on complexity. We provide a detailed timeline during our free consultation.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes. We build most websites with a content management system (CMS) so you can edit text, images, and blog posts without any coding knowledge. We also provide a training session after launch.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

All packages include post-launch support (1–6 months depending on your plan). After that, we offer monthly maintenance plans starting from S$200/month covering updates, backups, security, and minor changes.

What platforms do you build websites on?

We use modern frameworks like Astro, Next.js, and WordPress depending on your needs. For ecommerce, we work with Shopify and WooCommerce. We recommend the best platform for your specific requirements during the consultation.

Do you provide hosting?

We help set up hosting on reliable platforms like Cloudflare, Vercel, or managed WordPress hosting with Singapore-based servers for fast load times. Hosting costs are separate and typically range from S$10–S$50 per month.
