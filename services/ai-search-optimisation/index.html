FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Search Optimisation (GEO)?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) is the practice of optimising your brand's online presence so that AI platforms like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, and Microsoft Copilot recommend your business when users ask for suggestions. Unlike traditional SEO which helps you rank in search results, GEO ensures you're the brand AI names in its answers.

Is this different from SEO?

Yes. SEO gets you found in search results. GEO gets you recommended in AI answers. The overlap between Google rankings and AI citations has dropped below 20% — ranking #1 on Google no longer guarantees AI will recommend you. GEO complements SEO but requires different optimisation strategies focused on entity clarity, structured data, content freshness, and third-party authority signals.

Which AI platforms do you optimise for?

We optimise for all major AI platforms: ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, and Claude. Each platform has different source preferences, so we tailor our strategy to maximise your visibility across all of them.

How long does it take to see results?

Most businesses see measurable changes in AI citation rates within 30-90 days. Quick wins like schema markup and llms.txt implementation can improve visibility within weeks. Authority building and content freshness programmes compound over time for sustained results.

How do you measure AI search performance?

We track Brand Visibility Score (how often your brand appears in AI answers), Citation Rate (percentage of relevant prompts that mention you), and Share of Voice (your mentions vs competitors). You get a monthly dashboard showing your performance across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.
