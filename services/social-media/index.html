FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How many posts will I get per month?

Our Essential plan includes 8 posts per month, Professional includes 12 posts, and Full-Service includes 20 posts. Each plan also includes varying amounts of Stories, Reels, and video content.

Can I approve posts before they go live?

Absolutely. We share a content calendar with all posts, captions, and visuals for your approval before scheduling. You can request revisions at any time before publishing.

Do you create video content like Reels?

Yes. Our Professional and Full-Service plans include Reels and short-form video production for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. We handle scripting, filming guidance, and editing.

Which social media platforms do you manage?

We manage Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and YouTube. Our Essential plan covers 2 platforms, Professional covers 3, and Full-Service covers all platforms relevant to your business.

How do you measure social media success?

We track engagement rate, reach, follower growth, website clicks, and enquiries generated from social media. Monthly reports show what content performs best and how social media contributes to your business goals.
