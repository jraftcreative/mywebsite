The Challenge

Your Website Is Your First Impression in Singapore

In Singapore's competitive market, your website is often the first touchpoint a potential customer has with your brand. An outdated or poorly designed website doesn't just look unprofessional — it actively drives customers to your competitors.

FAQ

Web Design Questions Answered

How much does web design cost in Singapore?

Professional web design in Singapore typically ranges from $2,500 to $10,000+ depending on complexity. Our Starter package begins at $2,500 SGD for a 5-page responsive website, while our Business and Premium packages include more pages, custom features, and advanced SEO.

How long does it take to design a website?

A typical website takes 2–4 weeks from kickoff to launch. Simpler sites can be ready in 2 weeks, while more complex projects with custom features or ecommerce may take 4–6 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during the discovery phase.

Do you design mobile-responsive websites?

Every website we build is mobile-first and fully responsive. With over 70% of Singapore's web traffic coming from mobile devices, responsive design is essential — not optional. All our sites are tested across devices and browsers.

Will my website be optimised for Google?

Yes. Every website includes on-page SEO fundamentals — proper heading structure, meta tags, image optimisation, fast loading speeds, and schema markup. For ongoing SEO, we offer dedicated SEO plans starting from $800/month.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

Absolutely. Our Business and Premium packages include a content management system (CMS) so you can update text, images, and blog posts without any coding knowledge. We also provide training and documentation.
