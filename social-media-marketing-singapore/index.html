FAQ

Social Media Marketing — Questions Answered

How much does social media marketing cost in Singapore?

Social media marketing in Singapore typically ranges from $2,500 to $5,000+ per month depending on scope. Our Essential plan starts at $2,500 SGD/month for 8 posts across 2 platforms, while our Professional and Full-Service plans include more content, Reels, and platform coverage.

Which social media platforms do you manage?

We manage all major platforms including Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and YouTube. Our team selects the best platforms for your business based on your target audience, industry, and goals. Most Singapore businesses see the strongest results on Instagram and TikTok.

Do I get to approve content before it goes live?

Absolutely. Every piece of content goes through our structured approval workflow before publishing. You receive a content calendar in advance with all post designs, captions, and hashtags for your review. Nothing goes live without your sign-off.

Do your plans include video and Reels production?

Yes. Our Professional plan includes 4 Reels per month and our Full-Service plan includes 8+ Reels per month. All video content is produced, edited, and optimised for each platform. Short-form video is the highest-performing format on Instagram and TikTok in Singapore.

How do you measure the success of social media marketing?

We track metrics that matter — reach, engagement rate, follower growth, website traffic from social, and conversions. Every plan includes regular reporting with clear insights and recommendations. We focus on business outcomes, not vanity metrics.
