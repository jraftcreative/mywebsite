The Challenge

Competing for Enrolments in a Saturated Preschool Market?

Singapore has over 1,800 registered preschools, and parents now compare options online long before scheduling a tour. When a parent searches "preschool near Toa Payoh" or "Montessori kindergarten Singapore", they see a page full of options — and preschools without a strong digital presence get overlooked in favour of those running targeted ads.

Without a proper advertising strategy, your preschool relies on neighbourhood visibility and word-of-mouth while larger chains dominate Google search results and Facebook feeds. Open house events go under-attended, waitlists stay short, and available classroom spots go unfilled — all while competitors in the same estate run ads that capture the parents you should be reaching.

FAQ

Preschool Advertising — Questions Answered

How much does preschool advertising cost in Singapore?

Most preschools start with a monthly ad spend of $1,500–$5,000 on top of management fees. Google Ads for keywords like "preschool near me" typically cost $3–$10 per click, while Facebook ads targeting parents with young children cost $0.50–$3 per click. We help you allocate budget across both platforms for maximum enrolment impact.

Should my preschool use Google Ads or Facebook Ads?

Both work well but serve different purposes. Google Ads captures parents actively searching for preschools — high intent, ready to enrol. Facebook Ads reaches parents who may not be actively searching but fit your ideal profile — great for awareness, open house invitations, and building waitlists. We recommend running both for a full-funnel approach.

How do you promote preschool open house events?

We run dedicated open house campaigns 3–4 weeks before the event, combining Google search ads for parents actively looking with Facebook event campaigns targeting parents by child age, location, and interests. Landing pages capture RSVPs, and we retarget visitors who didn't register to maximise attendance.

Can ads help build a preschool waitlist?

Yes. We create waitlist-specific campaigns with lead generation forms on both Google and Facebook. Parents who submit their details are automatically added to your waitlist pipeline. This is particularly effective for popular preschools in estates like Bukit Timah, Holland Village, and Novena where demand exceeds available spots.

How do you target parents with preschool-age children?

On Google, we target search keywords parents use when looking for preschools. On Facebook, we use detailed demographic targeting — parents with children aged 18 months to 6 years, in specific neighbourhoods, with relevant interests. We also build lookalike audiences from your existing parent database to find similar families in Singapore.
