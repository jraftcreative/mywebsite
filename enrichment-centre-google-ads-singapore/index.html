The Challenge

Struggling to Stand Out in Singapore's Crowded Enrichment Market?

Singapore parents spend heavily on enrichment programmes, but with hundreds of centres offering everything from robotics to piano lessons, getting noticed is harder than ever. Parents turn to Google first — searching "coding class kids Singapore" or "art enrichment near me" — and if your centre doesn't appear, they'll book with a competitor who does.

Without targeted Google Ads, your enrichment centre relies on walk-ins and referrals while competitors capture every parent searching online. Generic social media posts don't reach parents at the moment of intent, and without conversion tracking, you have no way to measure which marketing efforts actually fill class spots.

FAQ

Enrichment Centre Google Ads — Questions Answered

How much should enrichment centres spend on Google Ads in Singapore?

Most enrichment centres start with a monthly ad spend of $1,000–$3,500 on top of management fees. Keywords like "coding class for kids Singapore" and "art enrichment near me" typically cost $1.50–$6 per click. We help you set the right budget during a free strategy session based on your programmes and locations.

What types of enrichment programmes work best with Google Ads?

Any programme parents actively search for performs well — coding and robotics classes, art and music enrichment, speech and drama, sports programmes, and STEM workshops. We build separate campaigns for each programme type so budgets and messaging are tailored to the specific audience and search intent.

How do you target parents looking for enrichment classes?

We target parents through high-intent search keywords like "coding class kids Singapore" or "art enrichment Bukit Timah", combined with location targeting around your centre. We also use demographic targeting to focus on household income brackets and age groups most likely to enrol in enrichment programmes.

Can Google Ads help fill trial class spots?

Yes. We create dedicated campaigns for trial class promotions with ad copy highlighting your free or discounted trial offer. Landing pages are designed specifically to capture trial sign-ups, and we track every booking back to the keyword and ad that generated it so you know your exact cost per trial.

How quickly will I see enquiries from Google Ads?

Enrichment centre ads can generate clicks and enquiries within the first week. However, it typically takes 4–6 weeks of data and optimisation to reach peak performance. During this period, we refine keyword targeting, test ad copy variations, and adjust bids to achieve the lowest cost per lead.
