The Challenge

Students Are Searching — Can They Find Your Dance Studio?

Every week, thousands of people in Singapore search "dance studio near me", "K-pop dance class Singapore", or "ballet lessons for kids" on Google. They compare studios side by side, watching choreography videos, reading instructor bios, and checking class schedules. If your dance studio doesn't have a professional website, you're invisible to these potential students — and they're signing up for trial classes at your competitors instead.

An Instagram page or a listing on ClassPass alone is not enough. Prospective students want to see your full range of dance styles, understand each instructor's training background and performance credits, watch student showcases and recital highlights, and check available class slots — all before they ever message you on WhatsApp. Parents researching dance classes for their children are especially thorough, comparing syllabi, checking recital schedules, and reading reviews from other parents.

Dance studios that invest in a proper website with clear class timetables, compelling instructor profiles, video galleries, and easy trial booking consistently outperform those relying on social media or word of mouth alone. In a market as competitive as Singapore's dance scene — from established academies in Dhoby Ghaut to boutique studios in Bugis and Tampines — your website is the first impression that determines whether a student books a trial or scrolls past.

What We Build

What We Build for Dance Studios

Every dance studio website we deliver is built to convert visitors into enrolled students. We go beyond a generic template — your site will feature dedicated pages for every dance style you teach, instructor profiles that highlight training and performance credentials, a video gallery that captures your studio's energy, and a frictionless booking flow that makes it easy to sign up for a trial class.

A great website is just the starting point. Pair your dance studio site with local SEO services to rank for "dance studio near me" searches in your area, social media marketing to reach students on Instagram and TikTok, and Google Ads to capture high-intent searches during peak enrolment periods.

Dance Style & Class Pages

Dedicated pages for every dance style you offer — ballet, K-pop, hip-hop, contemporary, jazz, Latin, street, and more. Each page details class levels from beginner to advanced, age groups, session formats, and what students can expect, so prospective dancers find exactly the right class for them.

Online Trial Booking & Timetables

Let students book trial classes directly through your website. The booking system displays your weekly timetable, filters by dance style and instructor, and sends automated confirmation emails — cutting down WhatsApp back-and-forth and giving your reception team more time to welcome students at the door.

Instructor Profiles & Bios

Each instructor gets a profile page featuring their training background, performance credits, choreography highlights, and teaching philosophy. We include professional photos, embedded video clips of performances or classes, and certifications — giving prospective students full confidence in your teaching team.

Video Gallery & Recital Showcases

A dedicated gallery for choreography reels, student performances, recital highlights, and behind-the-scenes studio moments. Videos are embedded from YouTube or Vimeo and optimised for fast loading — letting visitors feel your studio's energy and teaching quality before they walk through the door.

FAQ

Dance Studio Website Questions Answered

How much does a dance studio website cost in Singapore?

A professional dance studio website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on features and complexity. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page website with a class timetable, dance style overview, contact form, Google Maps integration, and basic SEO. Business and Premium packages add online trial booking, instructor profiles, video galleries, membership pricing, recital showcases, and online payment integration.

Can you add online class booking to our dance studio website?

Yes. Our Business and Premium packages include an online booking system that lets students register for trial classes, regular sessions, and workshops directly through your website. The system displays class schedules by dance style — ballet, K-pop, hip-hop, contemporary, jazz — shows instructor availability, and sends automated confirmation emails. This reduces phone calls and WhatsApp messages so your front desk can focus on welcoming students.

Can you build a video gallery for our dance studio website?

Absolutely. We build video galleries that showcase student performances, recital highlights, choreography reels, and instructor demos. Videos are embedded from YouTube or Vimeo and optimised for fast loading so your site stays quick even with dozens of clips. This is one of the most effective ways to convert visitors — prospective students want to see your teaching quality and studio energy before they walk in.

Can you help our dance studio rank on Google in Singapore?

Yes. We optimise your website for keywords students actually search — like "dance studio near me", "K-pop dance class Singapore", "ballet lessons for kids Singapore", or "hip-hop classes Orchard". This includes on-page SEO, schema markup for dance studios, Google Business Profile optimisation, and location-targeted content that helps you rank in local search results and Google Maps.

How long does it take to build a dance studio website?
