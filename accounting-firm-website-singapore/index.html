The Challenge

SMEs Compare Accounting Firms Online Before Making Contact

When a business owner in Singapore needs an accountant, they search Google. They compare three or four firms, scan each website for services offered, check credentials, and shortlist the ones that look credible. If your firm doesn't have a website — or has one that looks like it was built ten years ago — you're eliminated before you even get a chance to pitch.

Accounting is a trust-driven industry. Potential clients want to see ISCA membership, ACRA registration, your team's qualifications, and clear descriptions of what you actually do. A well-structured website communicates professionalism and competence in a way that a Facebook page or a listing on a directory site simply cannot.

Singapore has thousands of registered accounting firms competing for the same pool of SME clients. Whether you specialise in audit, tax advisory, bookkeeping, or corporate secretarial services, your website is the first impression that determines whether a prospect picks up the phone or moves on to the next firm on the list.

What We Build

What We Build for Accounting Firms

Every accounting firm website we deliver is purpose-built for professional services. We go beyond a generic template — your site will feature structured service pages for each practice area, prominently displayed credentials, team profiles that establish authority, and local SEO so prospects find you when searching for accountants in Singapore.

We understand that accounting practices need more than just a website. That's why we offer a full digital package: pair your new site with SEO services to rank for local accounting searches, social media marketing for thought leadership content, and paid ads management for lead generation campaigns on Google and LinkedIn.

Service Pages (Audit, Tax, Bookkeeping)

Dedicated pages for each practice area — audit & assurance, tax advisory, bookkeeping, corporate secretarial, and GST filing. Each page clearly explains the scope, process, and who it's for, so prospects can self-qualify before contacting you.

Credential Badges & Trust Signals

Prominently displayed ISCA membership, ACRA registration, CPA accreditation, and any other professional certifications. Trust badges, client logos, and testimonials positioned where they matter most — above the fold and on service pages.

Client Portal & Document Upload

A secure portal where clients can upload financial documents, download completed reports, and track the status of their filings. SSL-encrypted with role-based access controls to keep sensitive financial data protected and compliant.

Tax Resource Centre & Blog

A knowledge hub with articles on tax deadlines, IRAS updates, GST guidelines, and financial planning tips. Positions your firm as a thought leader, drives organic traffic from search engines, and gives prospects a reason to return to your site.

FAQ

Accounting Firm Website Questions Answered

How much does an accounting firm website cost in Singapore?

A professional accounting firm website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with service descriptions, team profiles, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add client portals, resource centres, and document management.

Can you build a client portal for our accounting firm?

Yes. Our Premium package includes a secure client portal where clients can upload documents, download reports, and communicate with your team. The portal uses SSL encryption and access controls to keep financial data secure and compliant.

Do you display ISCA and ACRA credentials on the website?

Yes. We prominently display your firm's professional credentials including ISCA membership, ACRA registration, and any other certifications. Trust signals like these are critical for accounting firms — potential clients need to see that you are properly qualified and registered.

Can the website help us rank for accounting-related searches in Singapore?

Every website includes basic SEO setup. Our Business and Premium packages include advanced SEO with keyword targeting for searches like "accounting firm Singapore", "tax filing services Singapore", or "corporate secretary Singapore". We also set up Google Business Profile and local schema markup.

How long does it take to build an accounting firm website?

Most accounting firm websites go live within 2-3 weeks. A 5-page site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with client portals or document management may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
