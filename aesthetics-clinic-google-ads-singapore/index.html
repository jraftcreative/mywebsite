The Challenge

Losing Patients to Clinics That Advertise Smarter?

Singapore's medical aesthetics market is projected to exceed S$1 billion annually, and new clinics open in Orchard, Novena, and the CBD every month. When a potential patient searches "Botox Singapore" or "aesthetic clinic near me", the clinics running targeted Google Ads capture those high-value consultations. If your clinic doesn't appear, patients book with a competitor who does — even if your doctors are more experienced.

Aesthetics advertising in Singapore comes with unique challenges. MOH regulations restrict what you can say in ads, making it difficult to differentiate without expert knowledge of compliant copy. On top of that, keywords like "dermal fillers" and "laser treatment" are expensive, and poorly managed campaigns can burn through budgets without generating a single consultation.

Without a dedicated Google Ads strategy, your clinic relies on referrals and organic search alone — leaving thousands of dollars in potential consultations on the table every month. Patients researching treatments online are ready to book; you just need to be visible at the right moment.

FAQ

Google Ads for Aesthetics Clinics — Questions Answered

How much do Google Ads cost for aesthetics clinics in Singapore?

Aesthetics clinics in Singapore typically start with a monthly ad spend of $2,000–$5,000 on top of management fees. Keywords like "Botox Singapore" and "laser treatment near me" can cost $3–$12 per click due to high competition. We help you allocate budget to the highest-converting treatments during a free strategy session.

Are Google Ads for aesthetics clinics MOH-compliant?

Yes. All our ad copy is written in compliance with MOH advertising guidelines for medical aesthetics in Singapore. We avoid prohibited before/after claims, unsubstantiated results, and misleading language — ensuring your campaigns run without policy violations or account suspensions.

Which aesthetic treatments should I advertise on Google?

We recommend starting with your highest-margin treatments that have strong search volume — typically Botox, dermal fillers, laser treatments, and body contouring. We analyse search data specific to Singapore to identify which procedures have the best cost-per-consultation ratio for your clinic.

How do you track consultations from Google Ads?

We set up conversion tracking to monitor form submissions, phone calls, WhatsApp enquiries, and online booking completions from ad traffic. Each consultation request is attributed to the specific keyword and ad that generated it, giving you clear visibility into your cost per patient acquisition.

Can you help my aesthetics clinic outrank competitors on Google?

Yes. We conduct thorough competitor analysis to identify gaps in their keyword coverage and ad strategies. By targeting underserved treatment keywords, crafting more compelling ad copy, and optimising landing pages for conversion, we help your clinic capture patients that competitors are missing.
