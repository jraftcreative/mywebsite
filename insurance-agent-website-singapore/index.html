The Challenge

Your Expertise Deserves More Than a Company Profile Page

Most insurance agents and financial advisors in Singapore rely on their agency's generic profile page, a LinkTree, or social media alone to establish their online presence. That might generate referrals from your existing network, but it does nothing to help new prospects find you when they search Google for "insurance agent Singapore" or "financial advisor CPF planning."

Without your own website, you have no control over your personal brand. You can't showcase your MDRT qualifications, specialisations in areas like critical illness or retirement planning, or the testimonials from clients who trust you with their family's financial security. You're invisible to the thousands of Singaporeans actively searching for insurance advice online every month.

In a market where trust is everything, a professional website with your MAS representative credentials, areas of expertise, plan comparison tools, and genuine client stories sets you apart from agents who rely on cold calls and roadshows. It gives prospects a reason to reach out to you directly — because they've already seen the proof that you know what you're doing.

FAQ

Insurance Agent Website Questions Answered

How much does an insurance agent website cost in Singapore?

A professional insurance agent website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with agent profile, MAS credential display, contact form, and Google Maps. Business and Premium packages add plan comparison tools, lead capture funnels, calculators, CRM integration, and multi-advisor support.

Will the website comply with MAS advertising guidelines?

Yes. Every insurance agent website we build follows the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) guidelines on financial product advertising. We include proper disclaimers, your representative number, and ensure all content meets MAS Notice on Recommendations on Investment Products and other relevant regulations. We stay up to date with MAS requirements so your website remains compliant.

Can you build plan comparison or premium calculator tools?

Absolutely. Our Business package and above include interactive plan comparison tables where visitors can compare coverage, premiums, and benefits side by side for products like term life, whole life, critical illness, and Integrated Shield Plans. We can also build premium estimate calculators that help prospects understand ballpark costs before reaching out to you.

How do you help insurance agents generate leads online?

We build dedicated lead capture funnels into your website — from needs analysis questionnaires and free consultation booking pages to downloadable guides on topics like CPF planning after 55 or Medishield Life top-up options. Every enquiry is routed to your email or CRM so no lead is missed. We also integrate WhatsApp for instant contact.

How long does it take to build an insurance agent website?

Most insurance agent websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A 5-page personal branding site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with plan comparison tools, calculators, and CRM integration may take 3–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
