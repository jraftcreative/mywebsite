The Challenge

A Neglected Website Is a Ticking Time Bomb

Most business owners launch their website and never think about it again — until something breaks. Outdated plugins get exploited by hackers. Expired SSL certificates trigger browser warnings that scare visitors away. Broken links and stale content quietly tank your Google rankings while you focus on everything else.

In Singapore, cyber threats targeting small and medium businesses are rising every year. A single security breach can expose customer data, destroy trust, and cost thousands in recovery. And even without a breach, a slow or glitchy website silently bleeds revenue as visitors bounce to competitors with faster, more reliable sites.

Website maintenance is not optional — it is the ongoing work that keeps your site secure, fast, and visible on Google. Without it, your website degrades from a business asset into a business liability. The businesses that treat maintenance as a priority are the ones that stay ahead.

FAQ

Website Maintenance Questions Answered

How much does website maintenance cost in Singapore?

Website maintenance in Singapore typically ranges from $300 to $1,200+ per month depending on the scope of work. Our Starter plan begins at $300 SGD/month for basic security updates and backups, while our Business and Premium plans include more frequent updates, performance tuning, and priority support.

What happens if my website goes down outside business hours?

All our maintenance plans include 24/7 uptime monitoring with automated alerts. If your site goes down, we are notified immediately and begin troubleshooting — even outside office hours. Business and Premium clients receive priority response with faster resolution times.

Can I cancel my maintenance plan at any time?

Yes. All our maintenance plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you into agreements.

Do you maintain websites built by other agencies?

Yes. We maintain websites built on most platforms and frameworks — WordPress, Shopify, Wix, custom-coded sites, and more. We start with a full audit to understand your tech stack, then set up monitoring and maintenance workflows tailored to your site.

What is included in a monthly maintenance report?

Every maintenance report covers uptime statistics, security scan results, backup status, any updates applied, performance metrics including page speed and Core Web Vitals, and a summary of content changes made during the month. Business and Premium clients also receive recommendations for ongoing improvements.
