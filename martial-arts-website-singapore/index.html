The Challenge

Prospective Students Are Searching — Can They Find Your Martial Arts School?

Whether it's a parent looking for taekwondo classes for their child or an adult searching for a BJJ gym to train at after work, the journey starts on Google. Searches like "martial arts near me", "muay thai class Singapore", and "kids karate Jurong" happen hundreds of times every week. Prospective students compare schools by checking class schedules, reading instructor credentials, reviewing belt grading structures, and looking for an easy way to book a trial. If your school doesn't show up — or your website doesn't answer these questions — they're walking into a competitor's gym instead.

A Facebook page or an Instagram account can showcase your training clips and competition wins, but they can't replace a website with structured information. Parents evaluating martial arts programmes for children want to see curriculum details, safety measures, coach-to-student ratios, and age-appropriate class breakdowns. Adults researching MMA or BJJ want to compare disciplines, check sparring policies, and understand membership pricing — all before they send a message.

Singapore has a thriving martial arts community, with gyms and academies concentrated across areas like Tanjong Pagar, Kallang, Bukit Timah, and Woodlands. With multiple schools offering similar disciplines within a few MRT stops of each other, your website is the competitive advantage that determines whether a prospective student books a trial or keeps scrolling.

What We Build

What We Build for Martial Arts Schools

Every martial arts website we deliver is built specifically for combat sports and traditional martial arts academies. We go beyond a generic fitness template — your site will have dedicated discipline pages, instructor credentials with competition records, belt and grading progression displays, online trial booking, and separate programme sections for kids and adults.

A website is the foundation, but visibility drives enrolments. Pair your martial arts site with local SEO services to rank for "martial arts near me" searches, social media marketing to grow your community on Instagram and TikTok, and Google Ads to capture high-intent searches from parents and adults ready to train.

Discipline Pages & Class Programmes

Dedicated pages for every discipline — Brazilian jiu-jitsu, muay thai, taekwondo, karate, judo, MMA, boxing, and wrestling. Each page explains what students will learn, class structure, required gear, training intensity, and who the programme is for. Separate sections for kids, teens, and adult programmes let each audience find exactly what they need.

Trial Class Booking & Timetables

Make it effortless for new students to book a trial. The booking system lets them pick a discipline, select a time slot, and receive an automated confirmation with what to wear and what to expect. Interactive timetables filter by discipline, level, and instructor — so both beginners and advanced practitioners find the right class instantly.

Instructor Profiles & Competition Records

Each coach gets a profile page featuring their martial arts lineage, competition achievements, teaching certifications, and coaching philosophy. Fight records, podium finishes, and national team selections build credibility. Prospective students — especially parents — want to know their instructor's background before they commit.

Belt Grading & Competition Results

Visual belt and grading progression pages show each rank, advancement criteria, and estimated timelines. A competition results section highlights your students' tournament achievements with event names, dates, and medal tallies — demonstrating the quality of your training programme and the competitive culture of your school.

FAQ

Martial Arts Website Questions Answered

How much does a martial arts website cost in Singapore?

A professional martial arts website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page site with discipline overview, class timetable, instructor bios, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add trial booking, belt grading displays, competition results, member portals, and multi-location support.

Can you build separate pages for each martial arts discipline?

Yes. We create dedicated pages for every discipline you teach — BJJ, muay thai, taekwondo, karate, judo, MMA, boxing, wrestling, and more. Each page covers what students will learn, class structure, required gear, belt or ranking progression, and who the classes are suitable for. This structure also helps with SEO, so your school ranks for discipline-specific searches like "muay thai class Singapore" or "kids taekwondo Tampines".

Can you add a trial class booking system to our website?

Absolutely. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include an online trial booking system. Prospective students select their discipline, choose a class time, and receive an automated confirmation with details on what to wear and bring. This removes friction from the sign-up process and lets your coaching staff focus on instruction rather than answering booking enquiries.

Do you display belt rankings and grading systems on the website?

Yes. We build visual belt and grading progression pages that show each rank, the requirements to advance, and estimated timelines. This is particularly important for parents evaluating martial arts for their children — they want to see a structured curriculum with clear milestones before enrolling their child in a programme.

How long does it take to build a martial arts website?

Most martial arts websites go live within 2 weeks for our Starter package. Business-tier sites with trial booking, discipline pages, and competition results typically take 3 weeks. Premium builds with member portals, multi-location support, and event management take 4-5 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
