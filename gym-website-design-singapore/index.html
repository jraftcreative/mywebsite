The Challenge

Fitness Businesses Are Losing Members to Better Websites

Most people in Singapore search "gym near me" or "yoga studio near me" before ever stepping through the door. They expect to see class schedules, pricing, trainer credentials, and an easy way to book a trial — all from their phone. If your gym doesn't show up on Google, or your site is slow and hard to navigate, those potential members are signing up with a competitor instead.

An Instagram page alone isn't enough. Social media is great for engagement, but it can't replace a proper website with structured information. Visitors want to compare membership plans, check class times for the week ahead, and book a free trial without sending a DM and waiting for a reply. A website gives you control over the full experience — from first impression to first visit.

Singapore's fitness market is crowded. Boutique studios, CrossFit boxes, big-box gyms, and yoga spaces are all competing for the same local audience. Whether you run a single studio in Tiong Bahru or a multi-location chain across the island, your website is the first thing most people see — and it needs to convert browsers into paying members.

What We Build for Gyms & Fitness Studios

Class Timetables & Schedules

Interactive weekly timetables that update easily — filtered by class type, trainer, or location. Members can check what's on today, tomorrow, or next week without calling your front desk or scrolling through Instagram stories.

Membership Signup & Payments

Let members choose a plan, enter their details, and start their membership directly on your website. We integrate with Stripe and other payment providers available in Singapore so you can collect recurring payments without manual invoicing.

Trainer Profiles & Certifications

Professional profile pages for each trainer — certifications, specialisations, training philosophy, and a personal bio. Members want to know who's leading their classes before they commit to a membership.

Trial & Class Booking

Make it easy for new visitors to book a free trial or reserve a spot in a class. Simple booking forms with date and time selection, automated confirmations, and reminders that reduce no-shows and keep your classes full.

FAQ

Gym Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a gym website cost in Singapore?

A professional gym website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with class schedules, trainer profiles, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add online booking, membership portals, and payment integration.

Can you integrate class booking into my gym website?

Yes. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include class booking integration. We can connect your website to platforms like Mindbody, Glofox, or Gymmaster, or build a custom booking system so members can reserve spots in classes directly from your site.

Do you build websites for yoga studios and CrossFit boxes too?

Absolutely. We build websites for all types of fitness businesses — gyms, yoga studios, CrossFit boxes, Pilates studios, martial arts academies, and personal training studios. Each site is tailored to your specific class types, pricing model, and brand identity.

Can members sign up for memberships on the website?

Yes. We can integrate membership signup and payment processing directly into your website. Members can choose a plan, enter payment details, and start their membership without needing to visit your location. We work with Stripe and other payment providers available in Singapore.

How long does it take to build a gym website?

Most gym websites go live within 2-3 weeks. A simple 5-page site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with booking systems or member portals may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
