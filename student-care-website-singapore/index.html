The Challenge

Parents Are Searching for After-School Care — Can They Find You?

In Singapore, dual-income families are the norm. Every year, thousands of working parents search for "student care near me" on Google, looking for a safe, enriching after-school environment for their children. They compare centres side by side — reading reviews, checking programme schedules, and evaluating enrichment activities. If your student care centre doesn't have a professional website, you're invisible to these parents, and they're registering their children somewhere else.

A basic listing on a school directory or a dormant Facebook page is not enough. Parents want to see your full daily programme, understand your approach to homework supervision, and know what enrichment activities you offer — from art and music to coding and sports. They want to see photos of your facilities, read testimonials from other parents, and confirm your centre is MOE-registered and SSDB-aligned, all before they ever visit in person.

Student care centres that invest in a proper website with clear programme information, transparent fee structures, and easy online registration consistently fill their places faster than those relying on word of mouth alone. In a market where parents have dozens of choices within a 2-kilometre radius of any primary school, your website is what separates your centre from the rest.

What We Build

What We Build for Student Care Centres

Every student care centre website we deliver is built to convert parent enquiries into confirmed registrations. We go beyond a generic template — your site will feature structured programme schedules, enrichment activity showcases, and a frictionless registration flow that makes it easy for busy working parents to sign up their children.

A great website is just the starting point. Pair your student care site with local SEO services to rank for "student care near me" searches in your neighbourhood, social media marketing to reach parents on Facebook and Instagram, and Google Ads to capture high-intent searches during registration season.

Programme Schedules & Enrichment Activities

Clear, structured daily timetables showing homework supervision hours, enrichment classes, outdoor activities, and meal times. Parents can see exactly what their child will be doing after school — from guided study to creative workshops and sports.

Online Registration & Waitlist Forms

Let parents register their children directly through your website. Capture student details, school information, pickup authorisation, dietary requirements, and emergency contacts in one streamlined form — reducing phone calls and paperwork for your admin team.

Location, Pickup Info & Nearby Schools

Embedded Google Maps with your centre's location, walking routes from nearby primary schools, and clear pickup and drop-off instructions. Parents need to know logistics before they commit — make it easy for them to see you're conveniently located.

Parent Testimonials & Facility Showcase

Showcase real testimonials from satisfied parents alongside photos of your classrooms, play areas, and study spaces. Visible social proof and a look inside your centre build trust and give hesitant parents the confidence to register.

FAQ

Student Care Centre Website Questions Answered

How much does a student care centre website cost in Singapore?

A professional student care centre website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on features and complexity. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page website with programme information, a contact form, Google Maps integration, and basic SEO. Business and Premium packages add online registration, enrichment activity showcases, parent portals, and multi-centre support.

Can parents register their children online through the website?

Yes. Our Business and Premium packages include online registration forms that let parents sign up their children directly through your website. Forms can capture student details, school information, pickup authorisation, dietary requirements, and emergency contacts — reducing your admin workload and making registration seamless for busy working parents.

How do you display programme schedules and enrichment activities?

We create structured, easy-to-read schedule pages that show your daily after-school programme, weekly enrichment activities, and holiday camp timetables. Parents can see homework supervision timings, enrichment classes like art, coding, or sports, and outdoor activities at a glance — so they know exactly what their child will be doing after school.

Will the website include Google Maps and help with local SEO?

Yes. Every package includes Google Maps integration showing your centre's location, nearby schools, and pickup and drop-off information. We also optimise your site for local search terms like "student care near me" and "after-school care [neighbourhood]" so parents searching in your area find your centre first. Business and Premium packages include Google Business Profile setup and advanced local SEO.

How long does it take to build a student care centre website?

Most student care centre websites go live within 2 weeks for our Starter package. Business-tier sites with online registration and enrichment showcases typically take 3 weeks, while Premium builds with multi-centre support and parent portals take 4-5 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting so you can plan around your school term schedule.
