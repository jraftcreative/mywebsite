The Challenge

Patients Are Researching Physiotherapy Clinics Online Before Booking

When someone in Singapore suffers a sports injury, needs post-surgery rehabilitation, or deals with chronic back pain, their first step is almost always a Google search. Queries like "physiotherapy near me", "sports physio Orchard", and "best physiotherapy clinic Singapore" generate hundreds of searches every week. Patients compare clinics by checking therapist qualifications, reading about treatment approaches, verifying insurance acceptance, and looking for a convenient way to book. If your clinic doesn't appear — or your website doesn't provide this information — those patients are booking with a competitor.

Healthcare is a trust-driven industry. Patients want to verify that your therapists are AHPC-registered, see their academic qualifications and clinical specialisations, and understand exactly what a treatment session involves before they commit. A social media page or a simple Google Maps listing can't provide the depth of information patients need to feel confident choosing your clinic over another.

Singapore's physiotherapy market includes hospital-affiliated practices, private clinics in medical suites, and neighbourhood physio centres. Whether you operate a single clinic in Novena Medical Centre or have multiple locations across the island, your website is the primary tool patients use to evaluate and choose a provider. Clinics with comprehensive, well-structured websites consistently attract more appointment bookings than those that rely on referrals alone.

What We Build for Physiotherapy Clinics

Every physiotherapy website we create is purpose-built for the healthcare industry. Pair your physiotherapy website with healthcare SEO services to rank for treatment-specific searches in your area, social media marketing to share patient education content, and Google Ads to capture high-intent searches from patients actively seeking physiotherapy treatment.

Treatment Pages & Clinical Specialisations

Dedicated pages for every treatment you offer — sports physiotherapy, post-surgery rehabilitation, chronic pain management, workplace injury recovery, paediatric physio, and geriatric care. Each page explains the condition, your treatment approach, expected recovery timelines, and session structure so patients know exactly what to expect before their first visit.

Online Appointment Booking

Let patients book appointments directly through your website. The booking system lets them select a treatment type, choose a therapist, pick an available time slot, and receive automated confirmation with clinic address and preparation instructions. This reduces phone calls and WhatsApp messages so your reception team can focus on in-clinic patient care.

Therapist Profiles & AHPC Credentials

Each physiotherapist gets a profile page featuring their AHPC registration status, university qualifications, postgraduate training, clinical specialisations, years of experience, and a professional bio. Displaying registered credentials is essential for healthcare websites in Singapore — it's the trust signal patients look for before booking with a new provider.

Insurance, Medisave & Clinic Location Pages

Clear pages explaining which insurance providers and corporate health plans your clinic accepts, whether treatments are Medisave-claimable, required documentation, and estimated out-of-pocket costs. Individual clinic location pages with addresses, operating hours, nearest MRT stations, and embedded Google Maps help patients find the most convenient branch.

Physiotherapy Website Questions Answered

How much does a physiotherapy website cost in Singapore?

A professional physiotherapy website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page site with treatment overview, therapist bios, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add online appointment booking, individual treatment pages, insurance and Medisave info, patient portals, and multi-location support.

Can you integrate online appointment booking into our physiotherapy website?

Yes. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include online appointment booking. Patients can select a treatment type, choose a therapist, pick an available time slot, and receive an automated confirmation. The system can sync with your clinic's scheduling software so your reception team doesn't need to manage bookings manually.

Do you display therapist credentials and AHPC registration on the website?

Absolutely. Each therapist gets a dedicated profile page featuring their AHPC registration status, academic qualifications, clinical specialisations, years of experience, and a professional bio. Displaying AHPC credentials is a key trust signal for healthcare websites in Singapore and helps patients verify that they're seeing a registered professional.

Can you include insurance and Medisave information on the website?

Yes. This information is one of the most searched topics for physiotherapy patients in Singapore and significantly reduces enquiry calls.
