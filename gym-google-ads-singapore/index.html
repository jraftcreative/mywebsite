The Challenge

Losing Members to Gyms That Advertise Better?

Singapore's fitness market is intensely competitive. From boutique studios to national chains, every gym is fighting for the same membership-ready audience. When someone searches "gym near me" or "best gym Singapore", they compare the gyms that appear in search results and map listings. If your gym doesn't show up, they sign up with a competitor — often one that's no better than yours, just more visible.

Without structured Google Ads, gyms burn budget on broad awareness campaigns that generate clicks but not sign-ups, send traffic to a homepage that doesn't highlight the specific offer being searched, and have no tracking to measure whether ad spend actually converts into paying members. Meanwhile, competitors with well-targeted campaigns are filling their floors at lower cost per acquisition.

FAQ

Gym Google Ads — Questions Answered

How much should gyms budget for Google Ads in Singapore?

Most gyms start with a monthly ad spend of $1,000–$4,000 on top of management fees. Keywords like "gym near me" and "gym membership Singapore" cost $1.50–$5 per click, while more specific terms like "CrossFit gym Tanjong Pagar" or "personal training Orchard" cost $2–$6. We set budgets based on your membership capacity and growth targets.

Do New Year campaigns really work for gyms?

January and early February are the highest-converting months for gym advertising globally, and Singapore is no exception. We launch pre-planned New Year campaigns in late December to capture resolution-driven searchers early. Combined with compelling trial offers and limited-time membership deals, January campaigns typically deliver the lowest cost per sign-up of the year.

How do you target people looking for gyms in my area?

We use radius targeting centred on your gym location — typically 3–5 km in central Singapore and up to 8 km in suburban areas. Combined with neighbourhood keywords like "gym Bukit Timah", "fitness centre Jurong", or "gym near Paya Lebar MRT", we ensure your ads reach people who can realistically visit your facility.

Can Google Ads promote specific gym services like personal training?

Yes. We create separate campaigns for each service — general memberships, personal training, group fitness classes, strength training, and speciality programmes like boxing or HIIT. Each campaign targets service-specific keywords with tailored ad copy and landing pages that speak to each audience.

How do you track whether Google Ads actually generate gym memberships?

We set up conversion tracking across membership enquiry forms, trial pass sign-ups, phone calls, and WhatsApp messages. Each lead is attributed to the specific keyword, ad, and campaign that generated it. Over time, we can calculate your exact cost per trial visit and cost per paid membership.
