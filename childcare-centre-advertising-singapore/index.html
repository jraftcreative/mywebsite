The Challenge

Empty Spots While Parents Search for Childcare Elsewhere?

Singapore parents plan childcare months in advance — researching centres near their home or workplace, comparing infant-to-caregiver ratios, and shortlisting options based on reviews and location. If your childcare centre doesn't appear when they search "infant care near Clementi" or "childcare centre Bishan", they'll tour your competitor down the road instead.

Without targeted advertising, your centre depends on signage and referrals while parents scroll past your listing in favour of centres with stronger digital visibility. Infant care spots — the most in-demand and profitable age group — go to centres that reach parents early in the decision process. Every unfilled spot is months of lost revenue that a well-targeted ad campaign could have captured.

FAQ

Childcare Centre Advertising — Questions Answered

How much does advertising cost for a childcare centre in Singapore?

Most childcare centres start with a monthly ad spend of $1,500–$4,000 on top of management fees. Google keywords like "infant care near me" and "childcare centre Singapore" typically cost $3–$8 per click, while Facebook ads targeting parents cost $0.50–$3 per click. We help you set the right budget during a free strategy session.

How do you target parents looking for infant care in Singapore?

We combine Google search targeting for keywords like "infant care near me" and "baby daycare Singapore" with Facebook demographic targeting focused on new parents, expecting parents, and parents with children under 18 months. Location targeting ensures ads reach families within a practical commuting radius of your centre.

Can Google Ads help fill infant care spots specifically?

Yes. Infant care has some of the highest demand in Singapore due to limited spots and long waitlists. We create dedicated campaigns targeting infant care-specific keywords and demographics, with landing pages that highlight your infant-to-caregiver ratio, facilities, and programme approach to convert searches into tour bookings.

What platforms should childcare centres advertise on?

Google Ads captures parents actively searching for childcare options, while Facebook and Instagram reach parents who match your ideal family profile through demographic and interest targeting. For childcare centres, we recommend both platforms — Google for high-intent searches and Facebook for awareness, tour promotions, and waitlist building.

How long before I see enrolment enquiries from ads?

Google Ads can generate enquiries within the first week as parents searching for childcare see your ads immediately. Facebook campaigns typically take 2–3 weeks to optimise as the algorithm learns your target audience. Full campaign performance usually stabilises after 4–6 weeks of data and ongoing optimisation.
