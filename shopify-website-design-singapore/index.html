The Challenge

Your DIY Shopify Store Is Costing You Sales

Too many Singapore business owners set up a Shopify store themselves, pick a generic theme, and wonder why sales never come. The store looks like every other template site, the checkout doesn't support PayNow or GrabPay, and product pages aren't optimised for search or conversions.

A default Shopify setup leaves money on the table. Slow page loads from bloated apps, confusing navigation that buries your best products, and a checkout flow that doesn't feel local to Singapore shoppers all add up to abandoned carts and lost revenue.

Shopify is a powerful platform, but only when it's set up properly. You need a store that reflects your brand, loads fast, ranks in Google, and makes it effortless for customers to find products and pay using the methods they trust. That takes more than dragging and dropping a theme.

What We Build

What We Build on Shopify

Every Shopify store we deliver is purpose-built for conversions — not just assembled from default templates. We customise themes, configure payment gateways, structure your product catalogue, and set up analytics so you can track every dollar of revenue.

A Shopify store is only as successful as the traffic it attracts. That's why we offer a full digital package: pair your new store with product SEO to rank for the searches your customers are making, Google Shopping ads to drive targeted traffic, and social commerce to sell directly through Instagram and Facebook.

Custom Theme Design

We design Shopify themes that match your brand and convert visitors into buyers. Whether we're customising a premium theme or building from scratch with Liquid, your store will stand out from cookie-cutter templates and deliver a polished shopping experience.

Payment Gateway Integration

We configure Shopify Payments and integrate PayNow, GrabPay, Stripe, and credit/debit card processing so your Singapore customers can pay the way they prefer. Local payment options build trust and reduce cart abandonment at checkout.

Product Catalogue & Collections

We structure your products with clear categories, collections, tags, and variants so customers can browse and filter easily. Optimised product pages with high-quality images, compelling descriptions, and schema markup help your products rank in Google Shopping.

Conversion Optimisation & Analytics

GA4 ecommerce tracking, conversion funnels, and Shopify analytics so you know exactly where revenue is coming from and where customers drop off. We A/B test layouts, optimise checkout flows, and remove friction points that cost you sales.

FAQ

Shopify Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a Shopify website cost in Singapore?

A professionally designed Shopify store in Singapore typically costs between $3,500 and $15,000+ depending on customisation and features. Our Starter package at $3,500 SGD includes theme customisation, a payment gateway, and up to 30 products. Growth and Shopify Plus packages add custom theme design, multiple payment gateways, unlimited products, and advanced integrations.

Can you integrate PayNow and GrabPay with Shopify?

Yes. We configure Shopify Payments and integrate local Singapore payment methods including PayNow, GrabPay, Stripe, and credit/debit card processing. Offering familiar local payment options reduces checkout friction and increases conversion rates for Singapore shoppers.

Do you build custom Shopify themes or use pre-built ones?

Both. Our Starter package customises a premium Shopify theme to match your brand, while our Growth and Shopify Plus packages include fully custom theme design built from scratch using Shopify Liquid. Custom themes give you complete control over layout, functionality, and performance.

Can you migrate my existing store to Shopify?

Yes. We handle full migrations to Shopify from WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, and other platforms. We transfer your product data, customer records, order history, and SEO settings. We also set up 301 redirects so your search rankings are preserved during the move.

How long does it take to launch a Shopify store?

Most Shopify stores go live within 2-4 weeks. A Starter store with up to 30 products can launch in 2 weeks, while custom-themed Growth builds take 3-4 weeks. Shopify Plus projects with advanced integrations and custom checkout typically take 5-6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline before starting.
