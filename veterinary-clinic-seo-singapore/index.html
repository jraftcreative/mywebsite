The Challenge

90+ Vet Clinics in Singapore — Can Pet Owners Find Yours?

Singapore's pet ownership rates have climbed steadily, with an estimated one in three households now caring for at least one pet. This growing demand has fuelled expansion across the veterinary sector — over 90 licensed clinics now operate island-wide, from large 24-hour hospitals in Bukit Timah and the East to boutique neighbourhood practices in HDB heartlands. For clinic owners, the competition for new patients has moved decisively online.

"Vet near me", "24 hour vet Singapore", "cat vet near me", "dog vaccination clinic" — these are searches happening every day from pet owners who need help now. Whether it's a routine check-up or an emergency at 2am, the first thing most pet owners do is open Google. The clinics ranking at the top of search results and appearing in the Maps 3-pack capture the majority of new patient registrations. Everyone else relies on walk-ins or referrals that don't scale.

The veterinary SEO challenge is compounded by the emotional urgency of many searches. A pet owner searching "emergency vet near me" at midnight will call the first clinic they find. If your 24-hour practice doesn't appear in that search, you've lost a patient who might have become a long-term client — and you've lost them to a competitor who invested in the SEO you didn't.
