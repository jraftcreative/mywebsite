The Challenge

Generic Marketing Agencies Do Not Understand Wellness

Singapore's wellness industry is projected to reach $3.5 billion by 2027. From yoga studios and pilates centres to physiotherapy clinics and mental health practices, the market is expanding rapidly — and so is competition. Every wellness business is fighting for visibility among health-conscious Singaporeans who increasingly research, compare, and book online.

Many wellness businesses hire general marketing agencies that treat them the same as an e-commerce store or a restaurant. These agencies do not understand the trust requirements of healthcare marketing, the community dynamics of fitness businesses, the seasonal patterns of wellness trends, or the specific search behaviour of people seeking health and fitness services in Singapore. The result is generic campaigns that waste budget and produce mediocre results.

Jraft Creative is different. We specialise in the wellness industry. We understand that a physiotherapy clinic requires different marketing sensitivity than a gym. That a yoga studio's member acquisition differs from a mental health practice's client journey. That TCM marketing needs to bridge traditional medicine with modern accessibility. This specialisation means faster results, less wasted spend, and marketing that actually resonates with your target clients.

FAQ

Wellness Marketing — Questions Answered

How much should a wellness business spend on marketing in Singapore?

Most wellness businesses in Singapore invest between $2,500 and $10,000 per month on marketing. A typical allocation covers website maintenance, SEO, Google Ads, and social media content. The right budget depends on your business's size, number of locations, and growth targets. Our Essential plan at $3,000/month is a strong starting point for single-location wellness businesses.

What types of wellness businesses do you work with?

We work with all segments of Singapore's wellness industry — yoga studios, pilates studios, gyms, physiotherapy clinics, TCM practices, mental health clinics, veterinary clinics, spas, nutrition consultancies, personal trainers, chiropractic clinics, and holistic health practitioners. Each segment has its own audience, competitive dynamics, and marketing approach, and we tailor every strategy accordingly.

How is a wellness-specialist agency different from a general marketing agency?

A general agency treats every client the same — generic templates, generic strategies, generic results. We specialise in wellness because the industry has unique characteristics: the trust required for healthcare marketing, the community dynamics of fitness, the seasonality of wellness trends, and the specific search behaviour of health-conscious Singaporeans. This specialisation means faster results because we already know what works in your industry.

How long before marketing generates results for my wellness business?

Paid channels like Google Ads can generate client enquiries within the first week. SEO typically takes 3–6 months to build sustainable organic traffic. Social media builds brand awareness and community over 2–3 months. Most wellness business clients see a meaningful increase in bookings and enquiries within the first 60 days of a full-service campaign.

Do you handle everything in-house or outsource to freelancers?

Everything is handled in-house — website design and development, SEO, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, social media content creation, content writing, and analytics. No outsourcing, no subcontractors, no white-labelling. One team manages your entire marketing strategy, which means complete alignment across channels and a single point of accountability for results.
