The Challenge

An Outdated Website Is Costing You Customers

If your website looks like it was built five years ago, visitors notice. Outdated design, slow load times, and clunky mobile experiences don't just hurt your brand image — they actively push potential customers toward competitors with better websites.

In Singapore, over 70% of users browse on mobile. If your site isn't mobile-friendly, you're invisible to the majority of your audience. Add poor SEO, broken links, and confusing navigation to the mix, and you have a website that works against you instead of for you.

A website redesign isn't just a facelift. It's a strategic overhaul that addresses the root causes of poor performance — from user experience and site architecture to technical SEO and page speed. The result is a website that ranks higher, loads faster, and converts more visitors into paying customers.

FAQ

Website Redesign Questions Answered

How much does a website redesign cost in Singapore?

Website redesign in Singapore typically ranges from $2,500 to $10,000+ SGD depending on the number of pages, complexity, and features required. Our Starter redesign package begins at $2,500 for up to 5 pages, while our Business and Premium packages cover larger sites with advanced SEO migration and custom functionality.

Will I lose my Google rankings during a website redesign?

Not with proper SEO migration. We map every existing URL, set up 301 redirects for any changed paths, preserve meta tags and heading structures, and submit the updated sitemap to Google Search Console. Our goal is to maintain — and improve — your existing rankings throughout the transition.

How long does a website redesign take?

Most website redesigns take 2-4 weeks from kickoff to launch. Simpler redesigns with fewer pages can be completed in 2 weeks, while larger sites requiring content migration, custom features, or ecommerce functionality may take 4-6 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during the discovery phase.

Can you migrate my existing content to the new website?

Yes. We handle full content migration — text, images, blog posts, and downloadable files. We audit your existing content, identify what to keep, update, or remove, and ensure everything is properly formatted and optimised on the new site. No content gets left behind unless you want it gone.

Will there be any downtime during the redesign?

No. We build your redesigned website on a staging environment while your current site stays live. Once the new site is tested and approved, we perform a seamless switchover — typically during off-peak hours. Your visitors will never see an under construction page.
