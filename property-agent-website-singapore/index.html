The Challenge

Your Listings Deserve More Than a Portal Profile

Most property agents in Singapore rely entirely on PropertyGuru, 99.co, and SRX to reach buyers and tenants. These portals work — but you're competing with thousands of other agents on the same page, paying for every listing, and building zero brand equity of your own.

Without your own website, potential clients can't find you when they search Google for "property agent in Bukit Timah" or "condo specialist District 10." You're invisible outside the portals, and every lead you generate belongs to the platform — not to you.

Singapore's property market rewards agents who build personal brands. A professional website with your CEA credentials, transaction track record, area expertise, and active listings tells prospective clients you're serious about your business — and gives them a reason to contact you directly instead of scrolling past to the next agent on PropertyGuru.

FAQ

Property Agent Website Questions Answered

How much does a property agent website cost in Singapore?

A professional property agent website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with agent profile, listing pages, contact form, and Google Maps. Business and Premium packages add property search, area guides, CRM integration, and multi-agent support.

Can you integrate property listings from portals like PropertyGuru or 99.co?

Yes. We can set up your website to display your active listings with details like price, floor area, tenure, and photos. Depending on your needs, we can build a manual listing management system you control directly, or integrate with third-party data feeds so your website stays in sync with your portal listings automatically.

Will the website meet CEA advertising guidelines?

Absolutely. Every property agent website we build includes your CEA registration number, agency licence details, and salesperson credentials displayed in compliance with the Council for Estate Agencies advertising guidelines. We stay up to date with CEA requirements so your website is always compliant.

Can you help me rank for area-specific property keywords?

Yes. Our Business package and above include dedicated area guide pages targeting keywords like "condos in District 9" or "HDB resale Tampines." We build these as SEO-optimised landing pages with local content, nearby amenities, and property market data to help you rank for the specific areas you cover.

How long does it take to build a property agent website?

Most property agent websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A 5-page agent profile site can be ready in as little as 2 weeks, while larger projects with property search, area guides, and CRM integration may take 3–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
