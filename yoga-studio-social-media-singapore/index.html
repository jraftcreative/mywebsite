The Challenge

Beautiful Classes Alone Will Not Fill Your Studio

Singapore has over 300 yoga studios — from boutique shalas in Tiong Bahru to large franchise operations in Orchard Road. ClassPass, online yoga platforms, and home practice apps have given students more options than ever. The studios that consistently fill classes are not necessarily the ones with the best teachers or the most beautiful spaces. They are the ones students find, follow, and feel connected to before they ever roll out a mat.

Social media is where that connection begins. A potential student scrolling through Instagram at lunch decides whether your studio feels right for them based on a 15-second Reel or a single post. If your feed is sparse, inconsistent, or looks like a schedule board with stock imagery, they scroll past. The studio that shows up with authentic class footage, instructor personality, and genuine community moments earns the trial booking. In a market where switching studios costs nothing, your social media is the reason students choose you — and keep choosing you.

FAQ

Yoga Studio Social Media — Questions Answered

How much does social media marketing cost for yoga studios in Singapore?

Social media marketing for yoga studios in Singapore ranges from $2,500 to $5,000+ per month. Our Essential plan starts at $2,500 SGD/month for 8 posts across 2 platforms, with Professional and Full-Service plans offering expanded content, Reels production, and dedicated community management.

What type of social media content works best for yoga studios?

The most effective yoga studio content combines instructor-led tutorials, class atmosphere videos, student progress stories, wellness tips, and behind-the-scenes studio moments. Reels showing pose breakdowns and short flow sequences consistently generate the highest engagement. Lifestyle content connecting yoga to Singapore's fast-paced work culture resonates strongly with the local audience.

Which social media platforms should yoga studios in Singapore focus on?

Instagram is the primary platform for yoga studios in Singapore due to its visual nature and strong wellness community. TikTok is growing rapidly for short-form yoga content. Facebook remains effective for community building, event promotion, and reaching the 35+ demographic. We recommend starting with Instagram and expanding based on your target audience.

How can social media help my yoga studio attract new students?

Social media builds awareness and trust before a potential student books their first class. Consistent content showcasing your teaching style, studio atmosphere, and community helps people overcome the intimidation factor. Targeted local campaigns, trial class promotions, and student testimonial content convert followers into first-time visitors. Most studios see measurable growth in trial bookings within 2 to 3 months of consistent social media activity.

Do you create video content for yoga studios?

Yes. Video is central to yoga studio social media. We produce Reels and short-form videos including pose tutorials, class previews, instructor introductions, studio tours, and student testimonials. Our Professional plan includes 4 Reels per month, and our Full-Service plan includes 8+ Reels per month. We handle filming direction, editing, music selection, and caption writing.
