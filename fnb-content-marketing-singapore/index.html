The Challenge

Your Competitors Are Ranking for the Searches Your Customers Make

Every day, thousands of people in Singapore search Google for food-related content: "best brunch spots in Singapore", "where to eat in Tanjong Pagar", "best Italian restaurant CBD". The businesses that produce content targeting these searches capture organic traffic from diners actively looking for somewhere to eat.

Without a content strategy, your website is static — a homepage and a menu that Google has no reason to rank above competitors. Content marketing gives Google fresh, relevant pages to index, builds your authority as a dining destination, and creates a compounding asset that drives traffic for months and years after publication.

Social media posts disappear in hours. Paid ads stop working when you stop paying. But a well-written blog article ranking for "best cafe in Tiong Bahru" keeps bringing new customers to your website every single day — for free. That's the power of content marketing for F&B.

FAQ

F&B Content Marketing Questions Answered

How much does content marketing cost for an F&B business?

F&B content marketing starts at $1,500 SGD per month for 4 blog articles with keyword research and a content calendar. Growth plans at $3,000/month add email newsletters and recipe content. Enterprise plans at $5,000+/month include video content, influencer collaboration, and comprehensive link building.

What kind of content works for F&B businesses?

The most effective F&B content includes "best of" food guides (e.g., "best brunch in Tiong Bahru"), recipe articles, chef profiles, behind-the-scenes stories, seasonal menu features, food trend articles, and neighbourhood dining guides. All content is SEO-optimised to drive organic traffic from food-related searches.

How does content marketing help my restaurant get more customers?

Content marketing builds organic search traffic over time. When your blog ranks for food-related searches, it brings potential diners to your website who are actively looking for dining options. It also builds brand authority, supports social media with shareable content, and earns backlinks that improve your overall SEO.

How long before content marketing shows results?

Blog content typically takes 2–4 months to rank and drive meaningful organic traffic. However, content also has immediate value for social media sharing, email newsletters, and brand building. The compounding effect of consistent content means results accelerate over time.

Do you create content for social media as well?

Our Growth plan ($3,000/month) and above include social media content repurposing — taking blog articles and turning them into Instagram carousels, short-form video scripts, and social media posts. For full social media management, we recommend pairing content marketing with our social media service.
