How We Work

A Simple, Transparent Process

From first call to launch in as fast as 2 weeks. No surprises, no jargon — just clear communication and quality work at every step.

01

Discovery

Every great project starts with understanding your business inside and out.

We start with a free consultation to learn about your business, goals, target audience, and competitors. This is where we ask the right questions — what does success look like for you? Who are your customers? What's not working with your current setup?

By the end of this phase, we'll have a clear project scope, timeline, and strategy tailored specifically to your business. No cookie-cutter templates — every project is built from the ground up.

What you get

  • Free 30-minute strategy consultation
  • Competitor and market analysis
  • Project scope and timeline
  • Transparent quote with no hidden fees
02

Design

Your brand deserves more than a template. We design something uniquely yours.

We create wireframes and high-fidelity mockups tailored to your brand identity, your audience, and your business goals. You'll see how your site looks and feels before a single line of code is written.

We work with you to refine every detail — layout, typography, colours, imagery — until you're completely satisfied. Most clients approve designs within 1–2 rounds of revisions.

What you get

  • Custom wireframes and page layouts
  • High-fidelity design mockups (desktop + mobile)
  • Brand-aligned colour palette and typography
  • Up to 2 rounds of revisions included
03

Build

Clean code, blazing performance, and pixel-perfect execution.

Once designs are approved, we bring everything to life with hand-written, performance-optimised code. Every site we build is mobile-responsive, SEO-ready, and tested across all major browsers and devices.

We don't use bloated page builders or generic WordPress themes. Our sites are built lean and fast — typically scoring 90+ on Google PageSpeed Insights right out of the box.

What you get

  • Fully responsive website (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • SEO-optimised structure and metadata
  • Google Analytics and Search Console setup
  • Performance optimisation (90+ PageSpeed score)
04

Launch & Support

Go live with confidence. We handle everything so you don't have to.

We deploy your site to fast, reliable hosting, configure your domain and SSL, and run final checks across all devices. You'll get a walkthrough of how to manage your content, plus documentation for anything you need.

After launch, we don't disappear. Every project includes 30 days of free support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. Need ongoing maintenance or marketing? We offer monthly plans to keep your site performing at its best.

What you get

  • Domain and hosting setup
  • SSL certificate and security configuration
  • Content management training and documentation
  • 30 days of free post-launch support
