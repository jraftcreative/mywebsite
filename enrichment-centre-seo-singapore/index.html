Singapore parents spend over $1.68 billion a year on enrichment and tuition. But with more than 1,600 centres competing, most parents never scroll past the first few Google results. If your enrichment centre doesn't rank for searches like "enrichment class near me," "coding class for kids Singapore," or "art enrichment Bukit Timah," you're invisible to the parents who are actively looking to enrol.

How long before my enrichment centre ranks on Google?

Most enrichment centres start seeing measurable ranking improvements within 3–6 months. Quick wins like Google Business Profile optimisation and fixing technical issues can show results in weeks. Can SEO help parents find my enrichment centre on Google Maps?

Yes. Local SEO and Google Business Profile optimisation are core parts of our enrichment centre SEO strategy. What keywords should an enrichment centre target for SEO?

Enrichment centres should target a mix of programme-specific keywords ("coding class for kids Singapore," "art enrichment Bukit Timah"), neighbourhood searches ("enrichment centre Tampines," "tuition centre Jurong East"), and intent-driven queries ("best enrichment classes for Primary 3"). We conduct thorough keyword research to identify the highest-value terms for your specific programmes and locations.
