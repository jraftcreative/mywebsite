The Challenge

Parents Are Searching — Can They Find Your Speech & Drama School?

Every week, thousands of parents in Singapore search "speech and drama classes near me", "public speaking for kids Singapore", or "LAMDA exam prep" on Google. They compare schools side by side, watching performance clips, reading instructor bios, and checking class schedules. If your speech and drama school doesn't have a professional website, you're invisible to these families — and they're signing up for trial classes with your competitors instead.

A Facebook page or an entry on a directory listing is not enough. Parents evaluating speech and drama programmes want to see the full range of what you offer — public speaking, creative drama, storytelling, musical theatre, debate, and exam preparation for LAMDA and Trinity. They want to know each instructor's background and teaching approach, watch clips from past performances and showcases, and check available class times for their child's age group — all before they ever contact you.

Speech and drama education is a growing segment in Singapore, driven by parents who recognise the importance of oral communication, confidence building, and presentation skills. Schools that invest in a professional website with clear programme details, compelling performance showcases, and easy trial class booking consistently outperform those relying on word of mouth or social media alone. In a market with established names and new entrants alike, your website is the first impression that determines whether a parent enrols their child or scrolls past.

FAQ

Speech & Drama Website Questions Answered

How much does a speech and drama website cost in Singapore?

A professional speech and drama school website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on features and complexity. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page website with programme information, a contact form, Google Maps integration, and basic SEO. Business and Premium packages add trial class booking, instructor profiles, performance video galleries, show calendars, and online registration with payment.

Can you include a video and performance showcase on our website?

Yes. Our Business and Premium packages include a dedicated performance gallery where you can showcase student recitals, drama productions, public speaking showcases, and storytelling performances. We embed videos from YouTube or Vimeo and optimise loading so the gallery stays fast on any device. Parents can see first-hand what your students achieve on stage — a powerful trust signal that drives enrolments.

Can parents register and pay for classes online through the website?

Yes. Our Premium package includes a full online registration system where parents can browse programmes by age group or skill level, select a term or holiday workshop, and pay securely online. The Business package includes trial class booking with automated confirmation emails. Both options reduce admin work and let parents sign up at any hour — not just during office hours.

Can you help our speech and drama school rank on Google locally?

Yes. We optimise your website for keywords parents actually search — like "speech and drama classes near me", "public speaking for kids Singapore", or "LAMDA exam prep Singapore". This includes on-page SEO, schema markup for educational services, Google Business Profile optimisation, and location-targeted content that helps you rank in local search results and Google Maps.

How long does it take to build a speech and drama school website?
