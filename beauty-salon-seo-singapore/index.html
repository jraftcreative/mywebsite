The Challenge

Hundreds of Salons in Singapore — Can Clients Find Yours on Google?

Singapore has hundreds of beauty salons competing for the same pool of clients. From facial studios in Orchard to waxing specialists in Bugis and lash bars in Tanjong Pagar, the competition is relentless. The problem is that most salons rely on Instagram and marketplace platforms like Fave or ClassPass to attract clients — platforms they don't own, can't control, and that charge a commission on every booking. Meanwhile, the salons that invest in SEO capture clients directly from Google at zero per-booking cost.

Google processes thousands of beauty-related searches in Singapore every month. "Beauty salon near me", "facial treatment Novena", "eyelash extensions Singapore", "waxing near MRT" — these are high-intent searches from people ready to book today. If your salon doesn't rank for these terms, you're paying for every new client through ads or marketplaces, or worse, losing them entirely to the salons that appear first in search results.

The beauty salons winning on Google have dedicated landing pages for each service they offer, optimised Google Business Profiles with dozens of five-star reviews, and blog content answering the questions potential clients search before they book. Without this SEO foundation, even a beautifully designed salon in a prime location remains invisible to the thousands of clients searching online every week.

Our SEO Services

SEO Built for Beauty Salons

Beauty salon SEO requires a specialist approach. Clients search by treatment type, neighbourhood, and urgency — creating dozens of keyword combinations that need dedicated pages and local signals. We build SEO strategies specifically for how Singapore residents research and choose beauty salons.

Pair SEO with a professionally designed beauty salon website, social media marketing for beauty content that drives engagement, or Google Ads to capture demand during peak festive seasons and promotion periods.

Technical SEO Audit

Complete crawl of your beauty salon website to find indexing gaps, broken links, missing structured data, slow page loads, and mobile usability issues. We fix the technical barriers stopping Google from ranking your salon for searches like "beauty salon near me" and "facial treatment Singapore".

Service-Specific Landing Pages

Dedicated, keyword-optimised pages for every service — facials, eyelash extensions, waxing, brow treatments, body scrubs, manicures. Each page targets the exact terms clients use, like "hydrafacial near Orchard", "lash lift Singapore", or "Brazilian wax Bugis".

Local SEO & Google Maps

Google Business Profile optimisation with correct beauty service categories, salon photos, client review management, and local citation building across Singapore directories. Designed to get your salon into the Google Maps 3-pack for every "beauty salon near me" search in your neighbourhood.

Review Management Strategy

A structured approach to generating and managing Google reviews. We help you build a consistent flow of authentic reviews from satisfied clients — the single most influential factor in local search rankings and in convincing new clients to choose your salon over a competitor.
