The Challenge

Running a Multi-Service Wellness Business Without a Unified Ad Strategy?

Singapore's wellness industry spans fitness, healthcare, beauty, and holistic wellbeing — and many businesses operate across multiple verticals. You might offer physiotherapy and pilates under one roof, or run a wellness centre that combines TCM, massage, and nutritional coaching. Each service has its own audience, its own keywords, and its own competitive landscape.

Without a coordinated Google Ads strategy, multi-service wellness businesses either spread their budget too thin across too many campaigns, or focus on one vertical while neglecting others. Generic wellness keywords attract broad but unfocused traffic, and without vertical-specific tracking, you can't tell which services generate the best return. The result is wasted ad spend and missed opportunities across your entire business.

FAQ

Wellness Google Ads — Questions Answered

How much should wellness businesses budget for Google Ads in Singapore?

Budgets vary by wellness vertical. Most businesses start with $1,000–$4,000 monthly ad spend on top of management fees. Fitness keywords cost $1.50–$5 per click, healthcare terms $3–$10, and beauty and spa keywords $2–$6. We set your budget based on your specific vertical, service mix, and growth objectives.

Can one Google Ads agency manage campaigns across different wellness verticals?

Yes — and there are advantages to working with one agency across verticals. We understand how fitness, healthcare, beauty, and wellbeing audiences overlap and differ, which prevents budget waste from cross-targeting. A single agency also enables consistent tracking, unified reporting, and strategic coordination across all your wellness marketing.

Which wellness services convert best through Google Ads?

Services with high search intent and immediate need convert best — physiotherapy appointments, dental check-ups, spa treatments, gym memberships, and beauty treatments. We also see strong results from trial offers, first-visit promotions, and package deals that lower the barrier for new clients to try your service.

Do you handle healthcare advertising compliance for wellness ads?

Yes. For any wellness service that falls under healthcare advertising regulations — physiotherapy, TCM, mental health, medical aesthetics — we ensure full compliance with Google's healthcare advertiser policies and Singapore's PHMC guidelines. Non-healthcare verticals like fitness and beauty follow standard ad policies.

How do you measure success across different wellness services?

We set up vertical-specific tracking — appointment bookings for healthcare, trial sign-ups for fitness, treatment reservations for beauty, and consultation requests for holistic services. Each vertical has its own KPIs and benchmarks, so you see clear performance data for every part of your wellness business.
