The Challenge

Clients Research Lawyers Before Making Contact

Potential clients don't pick a lawyer at random. They research firms online, compare practice areas, read lawyer profiles, and look for trust signals — all before ever making a phone call. If your website looks outdated or generic, prospective clients will move on to a firm that presents itself more credibly.

An unprofessional website undermines the trust you've spent years building. Clients looking for legal representation need confidence that your firm is competent, established, and attentive to detail. A poorly designed site — slow to load, hard to navigate, or missing key information — sends the opposite message.

Singapore's Legal Profession (Publicity) Rules add another layer of complexity. Your website must comply with restrictions on solicitation, qualification disclosures, and how case results are presented. You need a web designer who understands these rules and builds your site accordingly — not a generic agency that treats your firm like any other business.

FAQ

Law Firm Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a law firm website cost in Singapore?

A professional law firm website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on complexity. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with practice area pages, lawyer profiles, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add blogs, client portals, and advanced features.

Will my law firm website comply with the Legal Profession (Publicity) Rules?

Yes. We design law firm websites that comply with Singapore's Legal Profession (Publicity) Rules. This includes restrictions on solicitation, proper disclosure of qualifications, and appropriate presentation of case results. We review all content against current guidelines before launch.

Can you add a client portal to my law firm website?

Yes. Our Premium package includes a secure client portal where clients can access case documents, track matter progress, and communicate with your team. The portal uses SSL encryption and access controls to protect confidential information.

Do you help with SEO for law firm websites?

Every website includes basic SEO setup. Our Business and Premium packages include advanced SEO with practice-area-specific keyword targeting, legal schema markup, and Google Business Profile optimisation. We help you rank for searches like "corporate lawyer Singapore" or "family law firm Singapore".

How long does it take to build a law firm website?

Most law firm websites go live within 2-3 weeks. A 5-page site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with client portals or multi-language support may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
