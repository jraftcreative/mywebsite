The Challenge

Patients Are Searching for Physio — Are They Finding Your Clinic?

Singapore's physiotherapy market is growing as the population ages and sports participation increases. When someone searches "physiotherapy near me" or "sports injury clinic Singapore", they see a mix of paid ads, map results, and hospital listings. If your clinic isn't in that first page of results, you're losing patients to the clinic that is — whether it's the hospital chain down the road or a private practice with better online visibility.

FAQ

Physiotherapy Google Ads — Questions Answered

How much should physiotherapy clinics budget for Google Ads in Singapore?

Most physiotherapy clinics start with a monthly ad spend of $1,500–$4,000 on top of management fees. Keywords like "physiotherapy Singapore" typically cost $3–$8 per click, while condition-specific terms like "ACL rehab physiotherapy Singapore" may cost $4–$12 due to higher patient value. We set your budget based on treatment capacity and target patient volume.

Which physiotherapy keywords convert best?

Treatment-specific and condition-driven keywords convert best — "sports physiotherapy Singapore", "lower back pain treatment near me", "post-surgery rehab clinic", and "frozen shoulder physiotherapy". We also target insurance-related queries like "physiotherapy clinic Medisave approved" which attract patients ready to book.

Can Google Ads target patients with specific conditions?

Yes. We create separate campaigns for each treatment area — sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, chronic pain management, workplace ergonomics, and elderly mobility. Each campaign uses condition-specific keywords, ad copy addressing the patient's concern, and dedicated landing pages with relevant treatment information.

Do you handle healthcare advertising compliance for Google Ads?

Yes. We ensure all ad copy and landing pages comply with Google's healthcare advertising policies and Singapore's PHMC advertising guidelines. This includes avoiding prohibited claims, ensuring proper disclaimers, and structuring ads that Google approves without delays or disapprovals.

How do you track patient enquiries from Google Ads?

We set up comprehensive tracking across appointment booking forms, phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and online scheduling widgets. Every patient enquiry is attributed to the specific keyword and campaign that generated it, giving you clear cost-per-patient data to measure your return on ad spend.
