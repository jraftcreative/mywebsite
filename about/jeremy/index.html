Jeremy Cheong

Co-Founder & Managing Director, Jraft Creative

I started Jraft Creative because I saw too many small businesses paying premium prices for mediocre marketing — or worse, getting locked into long contracts with agencies that couldn't deliver results.

My approach is simple: every dollar you spend on marketing should drive measurable business growth. Whether that's a website that actually converts, SEO that brings in qualified leads, or paid ads with a clear return on investment.

What I Do

I personally oversee every project at Jraft Creative. My areas of expertise include:

  • Website Development — Custom-built sites using modern frameworks (Astro, React) with a focus on speed, mobile responsiveness, and conversion optimisation
  • SEO & Performance — Technical SEO audits, on-page optimisation, local SEO, and Core Web Vitals tuning
  • Paid Ads — Google Ads and Meta Ads campaign strategy, setup, and ongoing optimisation
  • AI Search Optimisation (GEO) — Getting brands cited and recommended by ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and other AI search platforms
  • Social Media Content — Strategy, content creation, and community management for Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Facebook

My Philosophy

I believe in transparency, speed, and ownership. That means no lock-in contracts, clear pricing, fast turnaround (most websites launch in 2 weeks), and you always own everything we create for you.

I also believe the best marketing doesn't feel like marketing. It feels like genuinely useful content, a beautiful website that's a pleasure to use, or an ad that actually answers the question someone was searching for.
