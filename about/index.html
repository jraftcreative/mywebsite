Our Story

Started With a Simple Belief

Today, we work with businesses across Singapore to build websites that convert, run campaigns that perform, and create content that resonates. No fluff, no vanity metrics — just measurable results.

Meet the Founder

Built on Real-World Sales & Marketing Experience

Jraft Creative wasn't born in a classroom — it was built on years of hands-on experience in sales and marketing across multiple industries in Singapore.

Before starting Jraft Creative, our founder spent years in high-growth sales and marketing roles, working closely with businesses to drive revenue, build pipelines, and scale operations. One of the most defining chapters: helping a company grow a business unit from zero to $2 million ARR in under a year.

But throughout that journey, one pattern kept showing up. Small businesses were constantly outgunned by bigger competitors with deeper pockets and dedicated marketing teams. And most business owners — stretched thin between operations, hiring, and cash flow — kept putting marketing off for "later." Later turned into months. Months turned into missed opportunities.

Jraft Creative was founded to close that gap. To give smaller businesses access to the same calibre of strategy, design, and execution that larger companies take for granted — without the enterprise price tag or the 12-month lock-in.

Today, Jraft Creative brings that same growth mindset to every client engagement — whether it's launching a new website, running paid campaigns, or getting your brand recommended by AI search engines.
