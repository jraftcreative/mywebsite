The Challenge

Organic Reach Alone Won't Fill Your Tables

You're posting beautiful food photos on Instagram and sharing updates on Facebook, but organic reach keeps declining. The algorithm shows your content to a fraction of your followers, and new customers rarely discover your restaurant through unpaid posts alone.

Meanwhile, your competitors are running targeted ads that appear in the feeds of food lovers living within a few kilometres of their restaurant. They're promoting weekend brunches, new menu launches, and delivery deals to people who are already interested in dining out — and they're filling their tables because of it.

Facebook and Instagram ads give you the ability to reach exactly the right audience at exactly the right moment. But poorly targeted campaigns with generic creative waste budget fast. Singapore's food scene demands ads that match the quality of the food — with precise location targeting, compelling visuals, and clear calls to action.

FAQ

Restaurant Facebook Ads Questions Answered

How much do Facebook Ads cost for a restaurant in Singapore?

Management fees start at $1,000 SGD per month, plus your ad spend budget. Most Singapore restaurants see strong results with a total monthly investment of $2,000–$4,000 including ad spend. Facebook's targeting options let you reach local diners cost-effectively even with modest budgets.

Which works better for restaurants — Facebook or Instagram ads?

We run campaigns across both platforms simultaneously through Meta Ads Manager. Instagram typically performs best for food photography and Reels content, while Facebook excels at event promotion and driving reservations for an older demographic. We optimise placement based on your restaurant's target audience.

Can you promote my restaurant's opening or special events?

Yes. Event promotion is one of the strongest use cases for Facebook Ads. We create dedicated campaigns for grand openings, seasonal menus, festive promotions, and special events — reaching people in your area who are likely to attend and building buzz before the event date.

How do you target people near my restaurant?

We use Meta's location-based targeting to reach people who live in, recently visited, or are currently near your restaurant's area. Combined with interest targeting for food, dining, and cuisine-specific audiences, we ensure your ads reach the people most likely to visit your restaurant.

Can Facebook Ads bring back previous customers?

Yes. We set up retargeting campaigns that show ads to people who have visited your website, engaged with your social media, or interacted with previous ads. This keeps your restaurant top of mind and encourages repeat visits — especially effective for promoting new menus or seasonal specials.
