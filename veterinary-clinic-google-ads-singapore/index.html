The Challenge

Pet Owners Are Searching — Is Your Clinic the One They Find?

Singapore has over 824,000 pet-owning households, and pet care spending grows year on year. When a dog stops eating, a cat needs emergency care at midnight, or a new puppy owner looks for their first vet, they start with Google. The clinics that appear in paid ads and map listings capture those pet owners — the clinics that don't appear lose them permanently to a competitor just a few kilometres away.

Without a structured Google Ads strategy, vet clinics rely on walk-in traffic and word-of-mouth while competitors run location-targeted campaigns that capture every pet owner searching nearby. The real cost isn't just one lost appointment — it's the lifetime value of that pet patient, the repeat vaccinations, the dental cleanings, and every friend that pet owner would have referred.

FAQ

Veterinary Clinic Google Ads — Questions Answered

How much should vet clinics budget for Google Ads in Singapore?

Most veterinary clinics start with a monthly ad spend of $1,000–$4,000 on top of management fees. General keywords like "vet clinic Singapore" cost $2–$5 per click, while emergency searches like "24 hour vet Singapore" cost $5–$12 due to urgency and competition. We allocate budgets across emergency and routine services based on your clinic's priorities.

Should vet clinics advertise emergency services on Google?

If you offer after-hours or emergency care, absolutely. Emergency vet searches like "24 hour vet" and "emergency animal hospital near me" have the highest conversion rates because pet owners need immediate help and will call the first clinic they find. We set up call-priority campaigns with location extensions so distressed owners reach you within seconds.

How do you target pet owners in my clinic's area?

We use radius targeting around your clinic — typically 5–8 km — combined with neighbourhood keywords like "vet clinic Bukit Timah" or "animal hospital Tampines". For routine services, pet owners almost always choose a conveniently located vet, so tight location targeting is essential for efficient ad spend.

Can Google Ads promote seasonal vaccination campaigns?

Yes. We create timed campaigns for annual vaccination reminders, puppy and kitten first-visit packages, and seasonal health screening promotions. These campaigns target pet owners at key moments in their pet's care cycle — building long-term patient relationships rather than one-off visits.

How do you track whether Google Ads generate actual vet appointments?

We set up conversion tracking across phone calls, online booking forms, WhatsApp messages, and Google Maps direction requests. Each conversion is attributed to the specific keyword and ad that generated it — giving you clear cost-per-appointment data so you know exactly which campaigns deliver the best return.
