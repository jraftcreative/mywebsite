The Challenge

Quality Web Design Should Not Break the Bank

Too many Singapore SMEs are priced out of professional web design. Large agencies charge $10,000 or more for a basic business website, padding proposals with unnecessary features and ongoing retainer fees that drain your budget month after month. For a small business trying to establish an online presence, those numbers are simply out of reach.

The alternative — DIY website builders — creates a different problem. Template-based sites look generic, load slowly, and lack the SEO foundations needed to rank on Google. Your visitors can tell the difference between a professionally designed website and one assembled from drag-and-drop blocks, and that perception directly affects whether they trust your business enough to enquire or buy.

What SMEs actually need is a middle ground: a professionally designed, mobile-responsive website built with proper SEO structure and fast load times — at a price that reflects the real cost of the work, not inflated agency overheads. That is exactly what we deliver at Jraft Creative.

FAQ

Affordable Web Design Questions Answered

How much does an affordable website cost in Singapore?

Our affordable website packages start from S$2,500 for a 5-page professional website. This includes mobile-responsive design, basic SEO setup, a contact form, and hosting guidance. Business packages start at S$5,500 for growing companies that need more pages and features, while Premium packages from S$10,000 cover advanced requirements like ecommerce and custom integrations.

What is included in your affordable web design packages?

Every package includes a professionally designed, mobile-responsive website with SEO fundamentals built in from day one. You get a custom layout tailored to your brand, a contact form, fast page load speeds, Google-friendly structure, and full ownership of your website files. There are no hidden fees, no monthly lock-in contracts, and no surprise charges after delivery.

Is cheap web design the same as affordable web design?

No. Cheap web design usually means template-based sites with no SEO, poor performance, and no support after launch. Affordable web design means you get a professionally built, custom website at a fair price. Our sites are coded for speed, structured for Google rankings, and designed to convert visitors into customers — without the inflated price tags that large agencies charge.

Do I own my website after the project is complete?

Yes. You own 100% of your website files, content, and design. We do not lock you into proprietary platforms or ongoing contracts. Once the project is delivered, everything belongs to you. If you ever want to move to a different hosting provider or work with another developer, you are free to do so without any restrictions or transfer fees.

How long does it take to build an affordable website?

Our Starter websites are typically delivered within 2 weeks from the time we receive your content and brand assets. Business packages take 3 to 4 weeks, and Premium projects with advanced features take 4 to 8 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during our initial consultation so you know exactly when to expect your site to go live.
