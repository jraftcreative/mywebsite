The Challenge

Homeowners Browse Qanvast — But They Want to See Your Portfolio

Homeowners in Singapore start their renovation research on platforms like Qanvast and Houzz. They browse through dozens of firms, shortlist a few, and then do what every serious buyer does — they Google your company name. If you don't have a professional website with a strong portfolio, you lose the enquiry to an ID firm that does.

A website without proper project galleries is a missed opportunity. Potential clients want to see your completed projects in detail — room-by-room walkthroughs, material choices, layout transformations. A generic site with stock photos and a contact form doesn't give them the confidence to reach out. Your work needs to be front and centre, presented in a way that reflects the quality you deliver.

Singapore's HDB and condo renovation market is fiercely competitive. Hundreds of ID firms are fighting for the same pool of homeowners. Your online presence needs to stand out — not just on listing platforms, but on Google search results, on your own domain, and on social media. A purpose-built website is the foundation that ties all of that together.

What We Build for Interior Designers

Every interior design website we deliver is purpose-built for the renovation industry. Project Portfolio Galleries

Filterable project galleries with high-resolution images, room-by-room breakdowns, and project descriptions. Organised by property type — HDB, condo, landed — so homeowners can find projects that match their own home.

Before & After Showcases

Interactive before/after sliders that let visitors drag to reveal the transformation. Nothing sells interior design work better than showing the raw space alongside the finished result — it builds trust and drives enquiries.

Consultation Booking

Built-in booking forms that let potential clients select a date, describe their renovation project, and schedule a site visit or virtual consultation — reducing the back-and-forth of arranging meetings through WhatsApp or email.

Design Process & Services Pages

Structured pages that walk homeowners through your design process — from initial consultation to handover. Clear service descriptions, package breakdowns, and pricing transparency that set expectations before the first meeting.

FAQ

Interior Design Website Questions Answered

How much does an interior design website cost in Singapore?

A professional interior design website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page portfolio site with project gallery and contact form. Business and Premium packages add before/after showcases, blog, consultation booking, and client portals.

Can you build a portfolio website that showcases our projects?

Absolutely. Portfolio presentation is the core of every interior design website we build. We create filterable project galleries with high-resolution images, room-by-room breakdowns, and before/after comparisons that let potential clients see the quality of your work before they enquire.

Do you include before and after galleries?

Yes. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include interactive before/after galleries with slider comparisons. These are one of the most effective conversion tools for interior design firms — homeowners want to see the transformation you can deliver.

Can clients book consultations through the website?

Yes. We can integrate consultation booking directly into your website. Potential clients can select a date and time, describe their project, and book a site visit or virtual consultation — reducing the back-and-forth of scheduling via email or WhatsApp.

How long does it take to build an interior design website?

Most interior design websites go live within 2-3 weeks. A simple portfolio site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with 3D galleries or client portals may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
