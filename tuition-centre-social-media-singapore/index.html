The Challenge

Parents Are Searching — But Are They Finding Your Centre?

Singapore parents are some of the most research-driven in the world when it comes to education. Before committing to a tuition centre, they scroll through Instagram pages, read Facebook reviews, and compare centres based on what they see online. If your social media looks inactive or generic, parents move on to the next option — even if your teaching quality is excellent.

The tuition industry in Singapore is fiercely competitive. From Orchard to Tampines, centres are fighting for the same pool of parents preparing their children for PSLE, O-Levels, and A-Levels. Without a consistent social media presence that showcases real results, shares valuable study tips, and builds trust through testimonials, you're invisible to the parents who matter most. Posting once a month with a stock image and a "registration open" caption is not a strategy — it's a missed opportunity.

FAQ

Tuition Centre Social Media — Questions Answered

How much does social media marketing cost for tuition centres in Singapore?

Social media marketing for tuition centres in Singapore ranges from $2,500 to $5,000+ per month. Our Essential plan starts at $2,500 SGD/month for 8 posts across 2 platforms, with Professional and Full-Service plans offering more content, video production, and community management.

Which social media platforms work best for tuition centres in Singapore?

Facebook and Instagram are the most effective platforms for tuition centres in Singapore. Facebook excels at reaching parents through community groups and targeted ads, while Instagram is ideal for showcasing student achievements and learning environments through visual content and Reels.

What kind of content do you create for tuition centres?

We create exam tips and study guides, student success stories and testimonials, behind-the-scenes classroom content, tutor introductions, programme highlights, and enrolment campaign posts. Every piece of content is designed to build trust with parents and position your centre as a credible educational partner.

How do you help tuition centres build trust with parents online?

We build trust through consistent, value-driven content — real student results, parent testimonials, transparent communication about teaching methods, and educational tips parents can use at home. This positions your centre as an authority and makes parents feel confident choosing you over competitors.

Can social media marketing help my tuition centre get more enrolments?

Yes. Strategic social media marketing drives awareness, builds credibility, and generates enquiries from parents actively looking for tuition in Singapore. We combine organic content with targeted campaigns around peak enrolment periods like post-PSLE, mid-year, and year-end registration windows.
