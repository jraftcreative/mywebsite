The Challenge

Your Nail Art Is Stunning — But Your Online Presence Isn't

Nail salons across Singapore post beautiful nail art on Instagram every day. But when a potential client searches "nail salon near Bugis" or "gel manicure Singapore" on Google, they don't find your Instagram page — they find the salons that have websites. Without a dedicated website, your nail art exists only in a feed that gets buried within hours, invisible to the clients actively searching for nail services in your area.

A professional nail salon website works around the clock. It showcases your full nail art portfolio in a browsable, filterable gallery — not a chaotic feed. It displays your service menu with transparent pricing so clients know exactly what to expect. It lets them book appointments online at 11pm on a Sunday night when inspiration strikes. And it shows up in local search results when someone types "nail salon near me" into Google Maps.

In a city where new nail studios open every month — from Japanese-style salons in Tanjong Pagar to Korean nail art studios in Orchard — standing out requires more than beautiful nails. It requires a website that works as hard as your nail technicians do.

What We Build

What We Build for Nail Salons

Every nail salon website we create is built around the visual nature of your work. We design gallery-driven sites that showcase your nail art front and centre, paired with booking flows and gift card systems that convert admirers into paying clients.

Beyond websites, we offer a complete digital package. Pair your site with SEO services to rank for local nail searches, social media marketing for nail content that goes viral, and paid ads to promote festive season specials and new service launches.

Nail Art Gallery

Filterable, high-resolution galleries organised by style — gel nails, nail art, classic manicure, pedicure, extensions, press-ons. Clients browse your portfolio, save their favourites, and arrive knowing exactly what design they want.

Online Booking System

Seamless booking integration with Fresha, Booksy, or your preferred platform. Clients select their treatment, pick a nail technician, choose a time slot, and confirm — no messages, no waiting for replies, no missed appointments.

Gift Card & Voucher System

Digital gift cards that clients purchase directly on your website. Ideal for Chinese New Year, birthdays, Valentine's Day, and Christmas gifting. Recipients redeem online or in-salon — a proven revenue driver for nail salons in Singapore.

Instagram Feed Integration

Your latest Instagram posts displayed directly on your website. New nail designs appear automatically, keeping your site fresh without extra work and giving visitors a path to follow your salon for daily nail inspiration.

FAQ

Nail Salon Website Questions Answered

How much does a nail salon website cost in Singapore?

A professional nail salon website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with your service menu, nail art gallery, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add online booking, gift cards, and Instagram integration.

Can you add a nail art gallery to my website?

Yes. Every package includes a nail art gallery. Our Business package ($5,500) and above feature a filterable gallery where clients can browse your work by style — gel nails, nail art, manicure, pedicure, extensions — helping them decide what to book before they arrive at your salon.

Do you integrate online booking for nail salons?

Absolutely. We integrate with Fresha, Booksy, and other booking platforms used by nail salons in Singapore. Clients can pick their treatment, choose a nail technician, select a time slot, and confirm — all without leaving your website or making a phone call.

Can clients buy gift cards through my nail salon website?

Yes. Our Business package and above include a gift card or voucher system. Clients can purchase digital gift cards for friends and family directly on your website — a popular option during festive seasons like Chinese New Year, Christmas, and Valentine's Day in Singapore.

How long does it take to build a nail salon website?

Most nail salon websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A Starter site can be ready in 2 weeks, while projects with online booking, gallery features, or gift card systems typically take 3–4 weeks. We share a detailed timeline before starting work.
