The Challenge

Outstanding Treatments Mean Nothing If No One Finds You

Singapore's beauty industry is saturated. From HDB neighbourhood salons to premium Orchard Road studios, over 5,000 beauty businesses compete for the same customers. Walk-in traffic and word-of-mouth referrals alone are no longer enough — the majority of Singaporeans now search Google or scroll Instagram before choosing where to book their next facial, wax, or lash treatment.

Most salon owners know they need a digital presence, but managing SEO, Google Ads, social media, and online reviews across multiple platforms is a full-time job. Posting sporadically on Instagram or running an occasional ad without a strategy wastes money and delivers inconsistent results.

A full-service marketing partner eliminates this guesswork. When SEO, paid ads, social content, and review management work together under one strategy, every marketing dollar compounds — your salon ranks higher, gets more clicks, earns more trust, and books more appointments.

FAQ

Beauty Salon Marketing Questions Answered

How much does beauty salon marketing cost in Singapore?

Beauty salon digital marketing in Singapore starts at $3,000 SGD per month for essential services including SEO and social media. Growth packages at $5,500/month add Google Ads and content creation. Enterprise packages at $10,000+/month include a dedicated strategist, influencer campaigns, and video production.

What digital marketing channels work best for beauty salons?

Instagram and TikTok drive visual engagement with before-and-after content. Google Ads captures high-intent searches like "facial near me" and "beauty salon Orchard." SEO builds long-term visibility, and Google Business Profile management drives local walk-ins. The most effective strategy combines all channels.

How do you help beauty salons get more bookings?

We drive bookings through multiple channels: Google Ads targeting people actively searching for beauty services, SEO so your salon ranks for local searches, social media content that showcases your treatments, and review management that builds trust. Every campaign is tracked to cost-per-booking.

Do you manage online reviews for beauty salons?

Yes. Review management is included in all packages. We set up automated review request workflows, monitor Google and social media reviews, and help you respond professionally to both positive and negative feedback. Strong reviews are one of the biggest trust signals for new customers.

Can you market a new beauty salon with no online presence?

Absolutely. We regularly launch marketing for new salons in Singapore. We start by building your Google Business Profile, setting up social media accounts, running grand opening campaigns, and targeting neighbourhood-specific keywords. Google Ads provides immediate visibility while SEO builds over time.
