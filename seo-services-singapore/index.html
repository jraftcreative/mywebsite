FAQ

SEO Services — Questions Answered

How much do SEO services cost in Singapore?

SEO services in Singapore typically range from $800 to $3,000+ per month depending on scope. Our Essential plan starts at $800 SGD/month for small businesses, while Growth and Enterprise plans offer more comprehensive strategies for competitive industries.

How long before I see results from SEO?

Most businesses start seeing measurable improvements in organic traffic within 3–6 months. Quick wins like fixing technical issues can show results in weeks. SEO is a long-term investment — the results compound over time and continue delivering value long after the initial work.

What's the difference between SEO and paid ads?

SEO builds free organic traffic that grows over time — once you rank, you continue getting clicks without paying per visit. Paid ads deliver immediate results but stop the moment you pause spending. Most Singapore businesses benefit from combining both strategies.

Do you guarantee first page rankings on Google?

No reputable SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings — Google's algorithm uses hundreds of signals. What we guarantee is a transparent, data-driven approach with monthly reporting so you can see exactly how your SEO investment is performing.

Can you do SEO for my existing website?

Yes. We can optimise most existing websites. Our process starts with a comprehensive technical audit to identify issues and opportunities. If your site has fundamental performance or design problems, we may recommend improvements first — our web development team can handle that too.
