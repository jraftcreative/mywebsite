The Challenge

Couples Are Choosing Wedding Vendors Based on What They Find Online

Singapore couples plan their weddings digitally. Before they walk into a bridal studio or schedule a venue tour, they've already scrolled through dozens of portfolios, compared package prices, and read reviews on Google and social media. If your wedding business doesn't show up in search results — or your website looks dated and disorganised — couples move on to the next vendor on their shortlist. Your portfolio and pricing need to speak for you before you ever meet a client face to face.

An Instagram feed or listing on a wedding directory isn't enough. Couples want a single destination where they can browse your full portfolio by style and venue, compare packages at their own pace, and submit an enquiry at midnight without waiting for a reply the next business day. They want to picture their own wedding through your past work — and your website is where that emotional connection starts.

The Singapore wedding industry is competitive. From Raffles Hotel to boutique garden venues in Dempsey, from luxury bridal ateliers on Orchard Road to independent planners working HDB solemnisations, every wedding vendor is fighting for the same pool of engaged couples. Whether you're a photographer, planner, florist, or venue operator, your website is the storefront that determines whether couples enquire or keep scrolling.

What We Build

What We Build for Wedding Businesses

Every wedding website we deliver is purpose-built for the bridal and events industry. We go beyond generic templates — your site will feature full-resolution portfolio galleries, structured package pages, client testimonials, and local SEO so couples searching "wedding planner Singapore" or "bridal studio near me" find your business first.

We understand that wedding businesses need more than just a website. That's why we offer a full digital package: pair your new site with SEO services to dominate wedding-related searches, social media marketing to showcase real weddings and build engagement, and paid ads management to drive enquiries from Google and social platforms.

Portfolio & Gallery Pages

Full-width, high-resolution galleries organised by wedding style, venue, or event type. We optimise every image for fast loading without sacrificing visual quality — so couples can browse your best work on any device and immediately picture what you can do for their special day.

Package & Pricing Pages

Clearly structured pages that lay out your service tiers, inclusions, add-ons, and starting prices. Couples want to self-qualify before they reach out — transparent pricing pages reduce unqualified enquiries and attract clients whose budget matches your services.

Enquiry & Booking Forms

Custom forms that capture event date, guest count, preferred package, venue, and budget range. Couples can request a quote or book a consultation directly from your website, with automated confirmation emails sent to both you and the client — no leads lost overnight.

Testimonials & Vendor Showcase

Dedicated sections for client testimonials, real wedding features, and preferred vendor partnerships. Social proof from past couples and cross-links to collaborating vendors build trust and position your business as a connected, established name in the Singapore wedding scene.

FAQ

Wedding Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a wedding website cost in Singapore?

A professional wedding business website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with portfolio gallery, package overview, and enquiry form. Business and Premium packages add client portals, online booking, and vendor directories.

Can you build a website for a bridal studio or wedding venue?

Yes. We build websites for all wedding industry businesses in Singapore — bridal studios, wedding venues, planners, photographers, florists, and caterers. Each site is tailored to showcase your services, past events, and pricing packages in a way that converts browsing couples into confirmed bookings.

Will the website include a portfolio gallery for past weddings?

Absolutely. Every wedding website we build features full-width, high-resolution portfolio galleries organised by event or style. We optimise all images for fast loading without compromising visual quality — critical for photography-heavy wedding businesses where visuals drive buying decisions.

Can couples submit enquiry forms or book consultations through the website?

Yes. We build custom enquiry forms that capture event date, guest count, preferred package, and budget range. Couples can request quotes or book consultations directly from your website, with automated confirmation emails sent to both you and the client.

How long does it take to build a wedding business website?

Most wedding business websites go live within 2-3 weeks. A simple 5-page site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with client portals or vendor directories may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
