The Challenge

Juggling Multiple Agencies and Freelancers?

Most businesses in Singapore end up working with a different vendor for every channel — one freelancer for the website, another agency for SEO, a separate team for ads, and yet another for social media. The result? Fragmented marketing that wastes time, money, and momentum.

Without a unified strategy, your branding is inconsistent across channels, campaigns contradict each other, and nobody takes ownership of the bigger picture. You're left coordinating between vendors instead of focusing on running your business — and your marketing budget suffers for it.

A full-service digital marketing agency solves this by putting one team in charge of your entire online presence. One strategy. One point of contact. Every channel working together toward the same business goals.

FAQ

Digital Marketing — Questions Answered

What does a digital marketing agency do?

A digital marketing agency handles all aspects of your online presence — from building and maintaining your website, to ranking it on Google through SEO, driving traffic with paid ads, and growing your brand on social media. Instead of hiring separate specialists or freelancers, a full-service agency like Jraft Creative manages everything under one roof.

How much does digital marketing cost in Singapore?

Digital marketing costs in Singapore vary depending on the services you need. Website design starts from $2,500 SGD, SEO plans from $800/month, Google Ads management from $1,000/month, and social media marketing from $2,500/month. Most businesses combine two or more services for the best results.

Do I need all digital marketing services at once?

No. We recommend starting with the services that will have the biggest impact on your business right now. For most Singapore businesses, that means a solid website and either SEO or paid ads. You can add social media or other services as your business grows — there's no pressure to sign up for everything at once.

How do you measure digital marketing results?

We track real business metrics — website traffic, keyword rankings, lead volume, cost per lead, ad spend efficiency, and social media engagement. Every client receives regular reports with clear data showing what's working and where we're investing your budget. No vanity metrics, no fluff.
