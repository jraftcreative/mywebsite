The Challenge

Parents Are Choosing Childcare Centres Before They Ever Walk Through Your Door

Singapore parents start their childcare search months — sometimes a full year — before their child needs a place. They search "infant care near me" or "childcare centre Punggol" from their phones during lunch breaks, compare fee schedules across tabs, and check whether your ECDA licence is current. If your centre doesn't show up on Google, or your online presence is a bare-bones Facebook page with outdated photos, you've already lost that family to the centre down the road that made it easy to find information.

A listing on the ECDA portal or a mention in a parenting forum isn't enough anymore. Parents want structured, reliable information they can review at their own pace: your programme levels from infant care through K2, daily schedules, teacher-to-child ratios, fee breakdowns including government subsidies, and real photos of your classrooms and outdoor areas. They want to submit an enrolment inquiry or join your waitlist at 10pm — not wait until Monday morning to call your front desk.

With over 1,500 licensed childcare centres across Singapore and new operators entering the market regularly, the competition for enrolments is real. Whether you run a single-centre operation in Bedok or a growing chain across the heartlands, your website is often the first — and most influential — touchpoint parents have with your brand. It needs to communicate safety, professionalism, and warmth in equal measure.

What We Build

What We Build for Childcare Centres

Every childcare centre website we deliver is purpose-built for the early childhood sector. We go beyond generic templates — your site will feature programme breakdowns by age group, daily schedule pages, fee schedule displays with subsidy information, ECDA licensing details, and local SEO so families searching "childcare near me" find your centre first.

We also offer a full digital package beyond your website: pair your new site with SEO services to rank for neighbourhood childcare searches, social media marketing to share classroom activities and build parent communities, and paid ads management to drive enrolment inquiries from Google and Facebook.

Programme & Daily Schedule Pages

Dedicated pages for every programme level your centre offers — infant care, toddler, playgroup, nursery, K1, and K2. Each page outlines the curriculum approach, daily schedule with meal and nap times, learning goals, and enrichment activities so parents understand exactly what their child's day looks like at your centre.

Online Enrolment & Waitlist Forms

Let parents register interest, submit enrolment applications, or join your waitlist directly from your website — any time, any day. Custom forms capture the child's age, preferred programme level, and start date, with automated confirmation emails that keep parents informed and reduce your admin team's manual workload.

ECDA Licence & Fee Schedule Display

We prominently showcase your ECDA licence number, SPARK certification rating, and government subsidy eligibility details alongside a clear fee schedule breakdown. Parents actively verify these credentials when shortlisting centres, and transparent pricing eliminates the back-and-forth that delays enrolment decisions.

Virtual Tours & Parent Testimonials

Photo galleries, embedded video walkthroughs, and curated parent testimonials that showcase your learning spaces, outdoor play areas, and classroom activities. For busy parents who can't visit during working hours, a virtual tour gives them the visual confidence to commit to scheduling a physical visit.

FAQ

Childcare Centre Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a childcare centre website cost in Singapore?

A professional childcare centre website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on the features you need. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with programme overview, enrolment form, and photo gallery. Business and Premium packages add virtual tours, daily schedule pages, parent portals, and online fee payment integration.

Can parents submit enrolment and waitlist applications through the website?

Yes. We build online enrolment and waitlist forms directly into your website. Parents can select their preferred programme level — infant care, toddler, playgroup, nursery, or kindergarten — submit their child's details and preferred start date, and receive automated confirmation emails. This reduces phone calls and walk-in inquiries for your front desk staff.

Can you display our ECDA licence and SPARK certification on the website?

Absolutely. We prominently display your ECDA licence number, SPARK certification rating, and any government subsidy eligibility details (CDA, AICCS) on your website. Parents actively check for these credentials when shortlisting childcare centres, and visible compliance builds immediate trust and differentiates you from unlicensed operators.

Will our centre appear in "childcare near me" searches on Google?

We build every site with local SEO fundamentals — location-specific keywords, Google Business Profile optimisation, structured data markup, and neighbourhood targeting. This helps your centre rank for searches like "childcare near Tampines," "infant care Bishan," or "childcare centre Woodlands" so nearby parents find you before your competitors.

How long does it take to build a childcare centre website?

Most childcare centre websites go live within 2-3 weeks. A 5-page Starter site is typically ready in 2 weeks, while Business and Premium projects with virtual tours, parent portals, or multi-centre setups take 3-4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before work begins so you can plan around your enrolment cycle.
