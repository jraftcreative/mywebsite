The Challenge

Patients Research Clinics Online Before Booking — Is Your Website Convincing Them?

Aesthetics treatments are high-consideration decisions. Patients research extensively before committing — reading doctor credentials, comparing treatment options, studying before/after results, and checking reviews. In Singapore, searches like "botox Singapore", "facial treatment near me", and "aesthetics clinic Orchard" happen thousands of times monthly. If your clinic doesn't have a professional website that addresses these concerns, patients will choose a competitor who does.

A well-designed aesthetics clinic website builds the trust that social media alone cannot. It presents your doctors' qualifications and experience in detail. It explains each treatment with the depth patients expect — procedure details, expected results, recovery time, pricing guidance. It displays before/after galleries that demonstrate real outcomes. And it makes consultation booking frictionless, so interested patients can take the next step immediately without calling during office hours.

Singapore's aesthetics market is increasingly competitive, with clinics concentrated in Orchard Road, Novena, Tanjong Pagar, and Raffles Place. Beyond competition, MOH advertising regulations mean your website content must be carefully structured to promote your clinic without making prohibited claims. A generic web agency won't know these rules — but we do, and we build your site to be both effective and compliant from day one.

FAQ

Aesthetics Clinic Website Questions Answered

How much does an aesthetics clinic website cost in Singapore?

A professional aesthetics clinic website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with treatment overview, doctor profile, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add detailed treatment pages, before/after galleries, and consultation booking.

Do you ensure MOH advertising compliance for aesthetics websites?

Yes. All aesthetics clinic websites we build follow Singapore's Ministry of Health (MOH) Healthcare Services Advertising guidelines. We avoid prohibited claims, ensure treatment descriptions use approved language, and structure content so your website promotes your clinic without violating advertising regulations.

Can you build before and after galleries for my aesthetics clinic?

Absolutely. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include a dedicated before/after gallery with interactive sliders. We organise results by treatment type — Botox, fillers, laser, HIFU, thread lifts — so potential patients can see real outcomes for the specific procedures they're considering.

Do you create individual doctor profile pages?

Yes. Doctor profiles are essential for building patient trust. We create professional profile pages featuring qualifications, specialisations, years of experience, and professional affiliations. For multi-doctor clinics, each doctor gets their own dedicated page with consultation booking linked directly.

How long does it take to build an aesthetics clinic website?

Most aesthetics clinic websites go live within 3–4 weeks due to the detailed treatment content and compliance review required. Starter sites with fewer pages can be ready in 2 weeks. We provide a clear timeline and work closely with your team to ensure all content meets MOH advertising standards.
