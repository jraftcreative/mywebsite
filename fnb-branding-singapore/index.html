The Challenge

Good Food Alone Won't Build a Loyal Following

Singapore's F&B industry is worth over $12 billion, with thousands of restaurants, cafes, bars, and food brands competing for the same customers. The businesses that thrive long-term aren't just the ones with great food — they're the ones with brands that people recognise, trust, and seek out repeatedly.

Think about the F&B brands you love in Singapore. They have a consistent look across their storefront, menu, packaging, social media, and website. That consistency didn't happen by accident — it's the result of intentional branding that creates recognition and trust at every touchpoint.

Without cohesive branding, your F&B business looks like every other generic food outlet. Your takeaway bags are plain, your social media lacks visual consistency, and diners can't tell your brand apart from the competition. In a market this crowded, that's a recipe for being forgotten.

FAQ

F&B Branding Questions Answered

How much does F&B branding cost in Singapore?

F&B branding in Singapore ranges from $3,500 to $15,000+ depending on scope. Our Starter package at $3,500 SGD covers logo design and basic brand guidelines. Business and Premium packages add menu design, packaging, signage, multi-outlet brand systems, and brand strategy.

What types of F&B businesses do you brand?

We work with restaurants, cafes, bars, bakeries, cloud kitchens, food halls, catering companies, and packaged food brands. Whether you're a single hawker concept or a multi-outlet F&B group, we create brand identities tailored to your specific business model and target audience.

How long does F&B branding take?

Starter packages take 2–3 weeks. Business packages with menu and packaging design take 4–6 weeks. Premium packages with full strategy and multi-outlet systems take 6–8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline before any work begins.

Can you create a brand system for multiple F&B outlets?

Yes. Multi-outlet brand systems are a core capability. We create flexible brand architectures that maintain consistency across outlets while allowing individual personality. This includes shared visual elements, outlet-specific adaptations, and comprehensive guidelines for staff and vendors.

Do you handle food packaging design?

Yes. Food packaging design is included in our Business package ($7,500) and above. We design takeaway containers, bags, cups, boxes, and labels that reinforce your brand identity. For packaged food products, we also handle retail packaging design with all required labelling.
