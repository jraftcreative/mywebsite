The Challenge

Singapore Parents Compare Preschools Online Before Visiting Any of Them

The typical Singapore parent visits 3 to 5 preschool websites before booking a single centre tour. They compare programme structures, check ECDA licensing status, read parent reviews, and look through classroom photos — all from their phone during a lunch break or after the kids are asleep. If your preschool doesn't have a website, or if your site loads slowly and looks like it was built a decade ago, you've already lost that parent to a competitor who invested in their online presence.

A listing on SgKindergartens or a Facebook page with sporadic updates isn't enough. Parents want to see structured programme breakdowns by level — Nursery 1, Nursery 2, Kindergarten 1, Kindergarten 2 — with clear information on learning outcomes, daily routines, teacher qualifications, and fee structures. They want to download registration forms or submit an enrolment inquiry online at midnight, not wait until Monday morning to call your office.

Singapore has over 1,800 ECDA-licensed early childhood centres, and new ones open every quarter. Whether you run a single-centre preschool in Punggol or a growing chain with branches in Bukit Timah and Tanjong Pagar, your website is the first touchpoint that shapes parent perception. It needs to communicate warmth, professionalism, and transparency — the same qualities you deliver inside your classrooms every day.

What We Build for Preschools

Every preschool website we deliver is designed specifically for Singapore's early childhood education landscape. We go beyond generic school templates — your site will feature virtual classroom tours, structured programme pages for each level, teacher profile sections, and online registration that parents can complete from any device.

Your website is just the starting point. Pair it with our SEO services to rank for "preschool near me" searches in your neighbourhood, social media marketing to share classroom highlights and build a parent community, and paid ads management to drive targeted enrolment inquiries during peak registration seasons.

Virtual Tours & Photo Galleries

Interactive virtual walkthroughs and curated photo galleries of your classrooms, outdoor play areas, meal preparation spaces, and learning corners. Parents who can't visit in person — especially expat families relocating to Singapore — get a genuine feel for your preschool environment before making their shortlist.

N1 / N2 / K1 / K2 Programme Pages

Dedicated pages for each programme level — Nursery 1, Nursery 2, Kindergarten 1, and Kindergarten 2. We lay out your curriculum approach, daily schedules, enrichment activities, learning milestones, and teacher-to-child ratios so parents can see exactly what their child will experience at every stage.

Online Enrolment & Waitlist Forms

Custom registration forms that capture the child's date of birth, preferred programme level, start date, and any dietary or medical requirements. Parents receive automated confirmation emails upon submission, and your admin team gets organised entries instead of scattered WhatsApp messages and handwritten forms.

Teacher Profiles & ECDA Compliance

Dedicated sections for teacher qualifications, years of experience, and teaching philosophy — giving parents confidence in the people caring for their children. We also display your ECDA licence number, SPARK certification status, and subsidy eligibility details so families can verify your credentials at a glance.

FAQ

Preschool Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a preschool website cost in Singapore?

A professionally designed preschool website in Singapore ranges from $2,500 to $10,000+ SGD. Our Starter package at $2,500 covers a 5-page site with programme overview, enrolment form, and photo gallery. Business and Premium packages include virtual tours, teacher profiles, parent portals, and multi-branch support.

Can parents register or join a waitlist through our preschool website?

Yes. We build online registration and waitlist forms directly into your website. Parents can select the N1, N2, K1, or K2 programme, enter their child's date of birth, upload required documents, and receive an automated confirmation — all without your staff needing to process paper forms or chase WhatsApp messages.

How do you display ECDA licensing and compliance information?

We create a dedicated section on your website that prominently shows your ECDA licence number, SPARK certification badge, teacher-to-child ratios, and any MOE kindergarten registration details. These trust signals are placed in high-visibility areas where parents naturally look when evaluating a preschool online.

Will my preschool appear on Google Maps and local search results?

Yes. We optimise your website for local SEO and set up or enhance your Google Business Profile so your preschool ranks in Google Maps and "preschool near me" search results. We also add location-specific schema markup to connect your website with your physical centre and improve visibility in your neighbourhood.

How long does it take to build a preschool website?

Most preschool websites are completed within 2 to 3 weeks. A straightforward 5-page site can go live in under 2 weeks, while larger projects with parent portal integration, virtual tours, or multi-branch setups typically take 3 to 4 weeks. We share a detailed project timeline before work begins.
