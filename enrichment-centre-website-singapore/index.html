The Challenge

Parents Search Online Before They Enrol

Singapore's enrichment industry is worth an estimated $1.68 billion, with over 1,600 centres competing for the attention of parents who are actively researching programmes online. If your centre doesn't have a professional website — or relies on a dated page with no class details and no way to register — parents will move on to a competitor that makes the process easier.

Parents searching for "enrichment centre near me" or "tuition centre in Tampines" expect to see your programme details, class schedules, instructor profiles, and pricing within seconds — on their phone. If your site loads slowly, looks unprofessional, or buries key information behind multiple clicks, they'll pick the next centre that shows them what they need upfront.

Whether you run a STEM lab, a language school, an arts studio, or a multi-subject learning centre, your website is the first impression most parents will have of your centre. It needs to answer their questions, build trust, and make enrolment as frictionless as possible.

FAQ

Enrichment Centre Website Design Questions Answered

How much does an enrichment centre website cost in Singapore?

A professional enrichment centre website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with programme listings, contact form, and Google Maps integration. Business and Premium packages add online enrolment, parent portals, and multi-branch support.

Can you add online class registration to my enrichment centre website?

Yes. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include online registration and enrolment form integration. Parents can browse programmes, select class timeslots, and submit enrolment requests directly through your website — reducing phone calls and walk-in admin for your front desk.

Do you help with enrolment landing pages for enrichment centres?

Absolutely. We build dedicated landing pages for specific programmes — whether it's a holiday camp, PSLE preparation course, or creative arts workshop. Each landing page is optimised for conversions with clear pricing, class details, instructor profiles, and a prominent enrolment CTA so parents can sign up without friction.

Will parents find my enrichment centre on Google?

We optimise your website for local SEO so you rank when parents search for "enrichment centre near me" or "tuition centre in Tampines." We also set up and optimise your Google Business Profile so your centre appears on Google Maps with reviews, photos, and contact details — making it easy for nearby parents to discover you.

How long does it take to build an enrichment centre website?

Most enrichment centre websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A simple 5-page site can be ready in as little as 2 weeks, while larger projects with online enrolment, parent portals, or multi-branch features may take 3–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
