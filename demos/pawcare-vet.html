What We Do

Comprehensive Pet Care

From wellness visits to complex procedures, we offer everything your pet needs under one roof.

General Checkup

Thorough nose-to-tail wellness exams to catch health issues early and keep your pet in peak condition.

Vaccinations

Core and non-core vaccines for dogs, cats, and rabbits following AVS Singapore guidelines.

Surgery

Sterilisation, tumour removal, orthopaedic, and soft tissue surgeries with advanced monitoring.

Dental Care

Ultrasonic scaling, polishing, extractions, and dental X-rays to protect your pet's oral health.

Grooming

Professional bathing, fur trimming, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and de-shedding treatments.

Pet Boarding

Climate-controlled suites with daily vet check-ins, playtime, and webcam access for peace of mind.
