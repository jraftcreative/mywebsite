The Challenge

Delivery Platforms Take 30% — SEO Gives It Back

Singapore's F&B industry is one of the most competitive in the world. With over 7,000 restaurants, thousands of cafes, and hundreds of bars, getting found online is no longer optional — it's survival. When someone searches "food near me" or "best [cuisine] in [neighbourhood]", the businesses at the top of Google capture the foot traffic and delivery orders.

Food delivery platforms like GrabFood and Deliveroo charge 25–30% commission on every order. That's money straight off your margin. SEO lets you rank for the same food delivery searches and drive orders directly to your own website, where you keep the full revenue. It's the most cost-effective long-term channel for F&B customer acquisition.

But most F&B websites are thin and unoptimised. A homepage, a basic menu, and a location page aren't enough to compete. Without dedicated landing pages for your cuisine, services, and locations — and without a properly managed Google Business Profile — your business is invisible to the thousands of hungry customers searching Google every day in Singapore.

Our SEO Services

SEO Built for Food & Beverage

F&B SEO is a specialist discipline. Customers search by cuisine, neighbourhood, occasion, price range, and dietary requirements — creating hundreds of keyword combinations. We build comprehensive SEO strategies tailored to how Singapore residents discover and choose where to eat, drink, and order.

Complete crawl of your F&B website to find indexing issues, broken links, missing structured data, slow load times, and mobile problems. We fix the technical barriers preventing Google from ranking your business for local food searches.

Content & Review Strategy

Blog content targeting food guides and "best of" searches, plus systematic review generation and management across Google, TripAdvisor, and food review platforms. More reviews and content mean higher rankings and more trust.

FAQ

F&B SEO Questions Answered

How much does SEO cost for an F&B business in Singapore?

F&B SEO in Singapore ranges from $800 to $3,000+ per month. Our Essential plan at $800 SGD/month covers technical SEO, on-page optimisation, and Google Business Profile setup. Growth and Enterprise plans add content strategy, review management, and multi-location SEO for F&B groups.

How long does F&B SEO take to show results?

Most F&B businesses see measurable ranking improvements within 3 to 4 months. Google Business Profile optimisation and technical fixes can produce quick wins in weeks. Location and cuisine-specific keywords typically rank faster than broad competitive terms.

Can SEO reduce my reliance on food delivery platforms?

Yes. By ranking for "food delivery near me" and cuisine-specific delivery searches, you can drive orders directly to your own website — avoiding the 25–30% commission that platforms like GrabFood and Deliveroo charge. SEO is a long-term strategy to own your customer relationships.

Do you handle SEO for F&B businesses with multiple outlets?

Absolutely. Multi-location F&B SEO is a core strength. We create unique pages for each outlet, manage individual Google Business Profiles, build location-specific citations, and ensure each location ranks independently in its own neighbourhood.

What types of F&B businesses do you work with?

We work with restaurants, cafes, bars, bakeries, cloud kitchens, food halls, catering companies, and food product brands across Singapore. Whether you're a single hawker stall or a multi-outlet F&B group, we tailor our SEO strategy to your specific business model.
