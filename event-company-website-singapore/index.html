The Challenge

Event Companies Are Losing Bookings Without a Professional Website

Corporate HR teams planning annual D&Ds, marketing managers sourcing launch event vendors, and couples searching for wedding planners all start with a Google search. If your event company relies on word-of-mouth referrals and a basic social media page, you are invisible to the majority of prospects actively looking for event services in Singapore. They will book the competitor with a polished website that showcases past events, outlines packages clearly, and makes it easy to request a quote.

Singapore's event industry — from MICE conferences at Suntec to gala dinners at Raffles Hotel — is fiercely competitive. An Instagram grid cannot display your full portfolio by category, present detailed service packages with transparent pricing, collect structured enquiry data, or highlight your venue partnerships. Every lead that reaches you through a DM instead of a proper quote form means more manual back-and-forth, slower response times, and a higher chance of losing the booking to someone who made the process easier.

Event planning is a high-trust purchase. Clients are committing thousands of dollars before they see any results. A professionally designed website with categorised portfolios, client testimonials, and detailed case studies builds the credibility needed to close those bookings — especially for corporate clients who need to justify vendor selection to their management team.

FAQ

Event Company Website Design Questions Answered

How much does an event company website cost in Singapore?

A professional event company website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with event portfolio, services page, and enquiry form. Business and Premium packages add quote request systems, testimonials, venue directories, and client portals.

Can clients request quotes directly through the website?

Yes. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include a detailed quote request form where clients can specify their event type, guest count, venue preference, date, and budget range. You receive structured enquiries directly to your inbox — no more chasing details over WhatsApp.

Do you build event portfolio galleries?

Yes. We build categorised event portfolio galleries — corporate D&Ds, weddings, product launches, MICE events — with high-quality images, event descriptions, and optional video embeds. Each portfolio entry can include venue details, guest count, and services provided so prospects can see exactly what you deliver.

Can the website showcase venue partnerships?

Yes. We build dedicated venue partnership pages or directories where you can highlight your preferred venues — hotels, convention centres, rooftop spaces, and outdoor locations across Singapore. Each listing can include venue photos, capacity details, and a direct link to enquire about that venue through your company.

How long does it take to build an event company website?

Most event company websites go live within 2-3 weeks. A 5-page portfolio site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with quote systems, venue directories, or client portals may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
