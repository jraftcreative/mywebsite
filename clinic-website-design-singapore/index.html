The Challenge

Patients Are Searching — Can They Find Your Clinic?

Most patients in Singapore search for "clinic near me" or "GP near Toa Payoh" before deciding where to go. If your clinic doesn't have its own website — or relies on a basic directory listing with no booking option — you're losing patients to the clinic down the road that made it easy to find them, read reviews, and book an appointment online.

Running a medical practice in Singapore also means navigating strict advertising regulations. The Ministry of Health's Private Healthcare Facilities Advertising Guidelines dictate what you can and cannot say online. A generic website template won't account for these rules, and getting it wrong can mean costly compliance issues — or worse, having your listing taken down.

Then there's patient data. Every contact form, booking request, and enquiry your website collects falls under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act. Your website needs proper consent mechanisms, secure data handling, and a clear privacy policy — not an afterthought checkbox. Whether you run a neighbourhood GP clinic, a specialist practice, or an aesthetic clinic with multiple branches, your website has to work harder than most.

FAQ

Clinic Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a clinic website cost in Singapore?

A professional clinic website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with contact form, Google Maps, and mobile-responsive design. Business and Premium packages add online booking, doctor profiles, patient portals, and multi-location support.

Will the website comply with MOH advertising guidelines?

Yes. We design every clinic website with MOH Private Healthcare Facilities Advertising Guidelines in mind. That means no unsubstantiated claims, proper use of qualifications, compliant before-and-after imagery for aesthetic clinics, and clear disclaimers where required. We work with you to ensure all content meets regulatory standards before going live.

Can you add an online booking system to my clinic website?

Yes. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include online appointment booking integration. We can connect your website to clinic management systems or set up a standalone booking flow that lets patients select a doctor, choose a time slot, and receive confirmation — reducing phone calls and no-shows.

Will my clinic appear on Google Maps after the website is built?

We optimise your website for local SEO and integrate Google Maps on your contact and location pages. We also help set up and optimise your Google Business Profile so your clinic appears in Google Maps and local search results when patients search for clinics near them in Singapore.

How long does it take to build a clinic website?

Most clinic websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A straightforward 5-page site can be ready in as little as 2 weeks, while larger projects with booking systems, patient portals, or multi-location features may take 3–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting work.
