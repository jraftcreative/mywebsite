The Challenge

Clients Are Searching for "Hair Salon Near Me" — Are You Showing Up?

Every month, thousands of people in Singapore search Google for "hair salon near me", "balayage Singapore", or "Korean perm Orchard". They're not browsing — they're ready to book. If your hair salon doesn't have a website that ranks for these searches, you're losing walk-ins and appointments to competitors who do. Relying solely on Instagram or word-of-mouth limits your reach to people who already know you exist.

A professional hair salon website does what no social media platform can. It displays your full service menu with transparent pricing, lets clients browse stylist profiles and choose who they want, shows before/after transformations that sell your expertise, and integrates directly with Fresha or Booksy so appointments flow straight into your calendar. Clients expect this experience — anything less, and they'll book with the salon that offers it.

Singapore's hair salon market is fiercely competitive. Whether you run a Korean-style salon in Somerset, a boutique colouring studio in Tiong Bahru, or a neighbourhood unisex salon in Tampines, your website is the first impression that determines whether a potential client walks through your door or books with someone else down the street.

FAQ

Hair Salon Website Questions Answered

How much does a hair salon website cost in Singapore?

A professional hair salon website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with your service menu, stylist listing, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add online booking, before/after galleries, and product e-commerce.

Can you integrate Fresha or Booksy into my salon website?

Yes. We integrate with Fresha, Booksy, Timely, and other booking platforms popular with hair salons in Singapore. Clients can browse available time slots, pick their preferred stylist, and confirm appointments directly on your website without switching to another app.

Do you build stylist profile pages for hair salons?

Absolutely. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include individual stylist profile pages with photos, specialisations, years of experience, and client reviews. These profiles help clients choose the right stylist and build trust before their first visit.

Will my hair salon website rank on Google?

Every website we build includes foundational SEO — optimised meta titles and descriptions, schema markup, fast load times, and mobile-friendly design. For salons wanting to rank for competitive terms like "hair salon near me" or "balayage Singapore", we recommend pairing your website with our monthly SEO service.

How long does it take to build a hair salon website?

Most hair salon websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A Starter site can be ready in 2 weeks, while larger projects with online booking integration, stylist profiles, and before/after galleries typically take 3–4 weeks. We share a clear timeline before kicking off.
