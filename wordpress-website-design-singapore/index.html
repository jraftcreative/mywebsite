The Challenge

Why Most WordPress Sites Underperform

WordPress powers over 40% of the web, but that popularity comes with a catch — most WordPress sites in Singapore are poorly built. Cheap themes packed with bloated code, dozens of conflicting plugins, and zero security hardening leave businesses with websites that are slow, vulnerable, and difficult to maintain.

DIY WordPress setups often start well but quickly spiral into problems. Plugin conflicts break layouts overnight. Core updates crash functionality. Brute-force attacks exploit weak login pages. And page speeds tank as more plugins get stacked on top of each other to fix issues that shouldn't exist in the first place.

A WordPress website built by a professional developer is a different experience entirely. Clean code, carefully selected plugins, proper caching, security layers, and a theme built specifically for your business — not a multipurpose template stretched beyond its limits. That's what separates a WordPress site that drives results from one that drains your time.

FAQ

WordPress Website Questions Answered

How much does a WordPress website cost in Singapore?

A professional WordPress website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on complexity and features. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page WordPress site with a premium theme, essential plugins, and basic SEO. Business and Premium packages add custom theme development, WooCommerce, advanced security, and third-party integrations.

Is WordPress still a good choice for business websites in 2026?

Yes. WordPress powers over 40% of all websites globally and remains the most flexible CMS for businesses of any size. It offers thousands of plugins, full content ownership, and the ability to scale from a simple brochure site to a full ecommerce store. The key is having a developer who builds it properly — with clean code, security hardening, and performance optimisation from the start.

Will my WordPress site be secure?

Security is a top priority in every WordPress site we build. We implement SSL certificates, firewall configuration, login protection, automatic backups, malware scanning, and regular plugin and core updates. Most WordPress security issues stem from outdated plugins and weak passwords — we eliminate both through proper configuration and ongoing maintenance.

Can you add WooCommerce to my WordPress site?

Absolutely. WooCommerce is the most popular ecommerce plugin for WordPress and we integrate it into our Business and Premium packages. We set up product catalogues, payment gateways including PayNow and Stripe, shipping calculators, inventory management, and order notifications — everything you need to sell online in Singapore.

Do you offer WordPress maintenance after launch?

Yes. We offer optional WordPress maintenance plans covering core updates, plugin updates, security monitoring, daily backups, uptime checks, and content changes. WordPress requires regular maintenance to stay secure and fast — our plans handle all of that so you can focus on running your business.
