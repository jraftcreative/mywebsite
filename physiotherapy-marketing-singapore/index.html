The Challenge

Referrals Alone Will Not Sustain Your Growth

Singapore has hundreds of physiotherapy clinics — from hospital-linked practices in Novena to boutique sports physio studios in the CBD. Add chain clinics, polyclinic physiotherapy, and home-visit services to the mix, and patients have no shortage of options. When someone searches "physiotherapy near me" or "sports injury clinic Singapore," the clinics that appear at the top of Google capture the majority of bookings.

Many physiotherapy clinics depend heavily on doctor referrals and word-of-mouth. While these channels are valuable, they are limited and unpredictable. A patient searching Google for "physiotherapy for frozen shoulder" at 10pm is not going to call their GP for a referral — they are going to book directly with whichever clinic appears first in search results and has a professional website with clear information about that condition.

Full-service marketing solves this by building visibility across every channel patients use to find physiotherapy — a professional website that explains your services and makes booking easy, SEO that ranks your clinic for condition-specific and location-based searches, ads that capture patients in acute pain who need immediate help, and content that positions your physiotherapists as the trusted authorities in their specialisations.

FAQ

Physiotherapy Marketing — Questions Answered

How much should a physiotherapy clinic spend on marketing in Singapore?

Most physiotherapy clinics in Singapore invest between $2,500 and $8,000 per month on marketing. A typical allocation covers website maintenance, SEO, Google Ads, and content marketing. The right budget depends on your clinic's size, number of branches, specialisations, and patient acquisition targets. Our Essential plan at $3,000/month is a strong starting point for single-location clinics.

How long before marketing generates new patients for my physiotherapy clinic?

Paid channels like Google Ads can generate patient enquiries within the first week — people searching for physiotherapy are often in acute pain and ready to book immediately. SEO typically takes 3–6 months to build sustainable organic traffic. Content marketing and social media build authority over 2–3 months. Most clients see a meaningful increase in appointment bookings within the first 60 days.

What marketing channels work best for physiotherapy clinics in Singapore?

Google Search is the highest-intent channel — patients searching for "physiotherapy near me" or "sports physio Singapore" are ready to book. Google Business Profile is critical for local visibility and reviews. Content marketing builds long-term organic traffic through patient education articles. Social media builds credibility and keeps your clinic top-of-mind. A professional website ties everything together.

Do you understand healthcare marketing regulations in Singapore?

Yes. We are familiar with the Singapore Medical Council guidelines and PHMC advertising regulations that apply to physiotherapy clinics. We ensure all marketing content avoids prohibited claims, uses appropriate disclaimers, and maintains the professional standards expected in healthcare marketing. All content is reviewed for compliance before publication.

Can you help market specialised physiotherapy services?

Yes. We create targeted campaigns for each of your specialisations — sports physiotherapy, post-surgical rehabilitation, paediatric physiotherapy, geriatric care, women's health physiotherapy, and workplace ergonomics. Each specialisation has its own audience, search behaviour, and marketing approach. We build dedicated landing pages, ad campaigns, and content for each service line.
