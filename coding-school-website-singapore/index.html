The Challenge

Parents Are Searching for Coding Classes — Can They Find Yours?

With MOE's push for computational thinking and the growing emphasis on STEM education in Singapore, demand for kids' coding and robotics classes has surged. Parents are actively searching Google for programmes that teach Scratch, Python, robotics, and AI — and they're comparing schools side by side before they ever make an enquiry.

A social media page or a listing on a class directory is not enough. Parents want to see your full course lineup, understand how classes are structured by age and skill level, and view real projects their children will build. They want to know your instructors' backgrounds, whether you offer trial classes, and what differentiates your curriculum from the dozens of other coding schools in Singapore.

Coding schools that invest in a professional website with clear course pathways, visible student work, and frictionless trial class booking consistently fill more seats than those relying on word of mouth or marketplace listings alone. In a market where new STEM programmes launch every month, your website is what separates a serious academy from a weekend hobby class.

What We Build

What We Build for Coding Schools

Every coding school website we deliver is built to convert curious parents into enrolled students. We go beyond a generic school template — your site will feature structured course pathways by age group, compelling instructor profiles, student project showcases, and a trial class booking flow that removes every barrier to sign-up.

A great website is just the starting point. Pair your coding school site with local SEO services to rank for "coding class for kids near me" searches, social media marketing to reach parents on Instagram and Facebook, and Google Ads to capture high-intent searches during holiday camp registration periods.

Course Pages & Age-Group Pathways

Dedicated pages for every course you offer — Scratch for ages 6-8, Python for ages 9-12, robotics workshops, AI fundamentals for teens. Parents can quickly find the right programme for their child's age and skill level without sifting through irrelevant information.

Trial Class & Holiday Camp Booking

Let parents book trial classes and register for holiday camps directly through your website. The booking flow captures the child's age, coding experience, and preferred schedule — giving you qualified leads instead of vague enquiries.

Student Project Showcase

Display completed student projects — Scratch games, Python apps, robotics builds, AI experiments — with screenshots, descriptions, and embedded demos. Nothing convinces a parent faster than seeing tangible work their child will be able to create.

Instructor Profiles & Credentials

Showcase your instructors' qualifications, industry experience, and teaching philosophy. Parents choosing between coding schools want to know who is teaching their child — especially for advanced courses in Python, robotics, and AI.

FAQ

Coding School Website Questions Answered

How much does a coding school website cost in Singapore?

A professional coding school website in Singapore costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on features and complexity. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page website with course overview, instructor profiles, a contact form, and basic SEO. Business and Premium packages add individual course pages, trial class booking, student project galleries, holiday camp sections, and multi-branch support.

Can you build an online class registration system for our coding school?

Yes. Our Business and Premium packages include online class registration that lets parents browse available courses by age group, select preferred timings, and book trial classes or enrol directly through your website. The system captures student details, coding experience level, and preferred programming language — reducing phone enquiries and manual admin work.

Can you add a student project showcase to our website?

Yes. We build dedicated project showcase sections where students' completed work — Scratch games, Python apps, robotics builds, or AI projects — is displayed with descriptions, screenshots, and even embedded demos. This gives parents tangible proof of what their children will learn and motivates current students to see their work featured on the school's website.

How do you help coding schools rank on Google in Singapore?

We optimise your website for keywords parents actually search — like "coding class for kids Singapore", "robotics course near me", or "Python class for children Singapore". This includes on-page SEO, schema markup for educational organisations, Google Business Profile optimisation, and location-targeted content for each branch or neighbourhood you serve.

How long does it take to build a coding school website?

Most coding school websites go live within 2 weeks for our Starter package. Business-tier sites with individual course pages, project galleries, and trial booking typically take 3 weeks, while Premium builds with learning pathways, holiday camp sections, and student portals take 4-5 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting so you can plan around your term schedule.
