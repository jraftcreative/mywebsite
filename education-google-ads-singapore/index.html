The Challenge

Losing Students to Competitors Who Advertise Better?

Singapore's education market is worth over S$1.1 billion in private spending alone, and every segment — tuition, enrichment, preschool, language, professional training — is intensely competitive. When parents or students search Google for "Math tuition Singapore" or "coding class for kids near me", they see a mix of paid ads and organic results. If you're not in either, you're invisible.

Without a structured Google Ads strategy, education businesses burn budget on broad keywords that attract irrelevant clicks, send traffic to generic pages that don't convert, and have no tracking to measure which campaigns actually drive enrolments. Meanwhile, competitors with well-optimised campaigns capture the students you should be teaching — at a lower cost per acquisition.

FAQ

Education Google Ads — Questions Answered

How much should education businesses budget for Google Ads in Singapore?

Most education businesses start with a monthly ad spend of $1,500–$5,000 on top of management fees. Cost per click varies by segment — tuition keywords typically cost $2–$8, preschool keywords $3–$10, and professional training keywords $5–$15. We help you set the right budget based on your specific segment and goals.

Which education segments benefit most from Google Ads?

Every education segment with search demand benefits — tuition centres, enrichment programmes, preschools, childcare, language schools, test preparation, professional development, and corporate training. The key is targeting the right keywords and matching ad copy to the specific intent of each searcher.

How do you handle keyword bidding for competitive education terms?

We use a tiered bidding strategy — aggressive bids on high-converting long-tail keywords like "PSLE Math tuition Bukit Timah" and controlled bids on broader, more competitive terms. This approach keeps your cost per enquiry low while maintaining visibility on the keywords that matter most to your business.

Do you optimise landing pages for education ad campaigns?

Yes. We review and recommend improvements to your landing pages to maximise conversion rates. This includes clear calls-to-action, testimonials, programme details, and mobile optimisation. A well-optimised landing page can reduce your cost per enquiry by 30–50% compared to sending traffic to a generic homepage.

How do you track conversions for education businesses?

We set up comprehensive conversion tracking including form submissions, phone calls, WhatsApp clicks, and trial bookings. Each conversion is attributed to the specific keyword, ad, and campaign that generated it. This data drives ongoing optimisation so your budget focuses on the campaigns delivering actual enrolments.
