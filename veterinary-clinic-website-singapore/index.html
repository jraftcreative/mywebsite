The Challenge

Pet Owners Are Choosing Their Vet Online

Singapore's pet ownership market has grown significantly in recent years. With more than 80,000 licensed dogs and a thriving cat and small-animal community, pet owners across the island are actively searching for veterinary care online. They compare clinic services, check vet credentials, read Google reviews, and look for emergency availability — all before picking up the phone or walking into a clinic.

Most pet owners today expect to find a vet's full service list, operating hours, emergency contact information, and appointment booking online. If your clinic's website is outdated, doesn't appear on Google, or lacks basic functionality like a mobile-friendly layout, you're losing clients to the vet clinic down the road that invested in a professional online presence.

Veterinary clinics in Singapore also face unique needs. Pet emergencies don't follow office hours — your website needs to clearly display after-hours and 24-hour emergency information. Pet owners want to know about your veterinarians' qualifications and areas of expertise. And with more clinics opening across HDB estates, shopping malls, and landed neighbourhoods, standing out online has never been more important for sustained growth.

What We Build

What We Build for Veterinary Clinics

Every veterinary website we deliver is purpose-built for animal care practices. We go beyond a generic template — your site will feature structured service pages for every treatment you offer, veterinarian profiles with credentials, emergency service information, online appointment booking, and local SEO so pet owners can find your clinic when they need you most.

A website is just the starting point. Pair your new site with SEO services to rank for searches like "vet clinic near me" and specific treatments, social media marketing to share pet care tips and build community trust, and paid ads management to drive appointment bookings from Google and social platforms.

Service Pages & Treatment Listings

Dedicated pages for general wellness check-ups, vaccinations, surgical procedures, dental care, dermatology, and emergency services. Each page clearly explains the procedure, what pet owners can expect, and how to prepare their pet — with clear calls to action for booking.

Veterinarian Profiles & Credentials

Professional profile pages for each veterinarian — qualifications, AVA registration, areas of specialisation (surgery, dermatology, exotic animals), years of experience, and a personal bio. Pet owners want to know who will be caring for their companion before they visit.

Online Appointment Booking

Let pet owners book appointments directly from your website — selecting their preferred vet, choosing a service type, picking a convenient time slot, and specifying their pet's details. Automated confirmations and reminders reduce no-shows and free up your reception team.

24-Hour Emergency & Pet Owner Resources

Prominent emergency sections with tap-to-call buttons, after-hours availability, and clear instructions for urgent situations. Plus, a pet owner resource library with vaccination schedules, post-surgery care guides, and seasonal health tips that position your clinic as a trusted authority.

FAQ

Veterinary Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a veterinary clinic website cost in Singapore?

A professional veterinary clinic website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with service pages, vet profile, and contact form. Business and Premium packages add online appointment booking, pet owner resources, emergency service portals, and multi-location support.

Can you integrate online appointment booking for my vet clinic?

Yes. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include online appointment booking integration. Pet owners can select their preferred veterinarian, choose a service type such as vaccination, dental cleaning, or general check-up, pick a time slot, and receive automated confirmation — reducing phone calls and making scheduling easier for both your staff and clients.

Can you include 24-hour emergency information on the website?

Yes. For clinics that offer after-hours or 24-hour emergency services, we build prominent emergency information sections — including a dedicated emergency page, tap-to-call buttons, operating hours with holiday schedules, and clear instructions for pet owners in urgent situations. This is included in all packages.

Do veterinary websites need to follow any regulations in Singapore?

Yes. Veterinary practices in Singapore are regulated by the National Parks Board (NParks) and the Singapore Veterinary Association (SVA). Veterinarians listed on your website should hold a valid practising certificate from the Agri-Food and Veterinary Authority (AVA). We ensure all content is professional and compliant with industry standards.

How long does it take to build a veterinary clinic website?

Most veterinary clinic websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A standard 5-page site can be ready in 2 weeks. Larger projects with online booking, pet owner resource libraries, or multi-location features may take 3–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
