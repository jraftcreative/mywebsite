FAQ

Small Business Website Questions Answered

How much does a small business website cost in Singapore?

A professional small business website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ SGD depending on the number of pages and features. Our Starter package at $2,500 includes a 5-page responsive website with a contact form and basic SEO — everything a new small business needs to get online.

Do I really need a website as a small business?

Yes. Over 80% of Singapore consumers research businesses online before making a purchase. Without a website, you're invisible to potential customers searching on Google. A professional website builds credibility, captures leads 24/7, and gives you a platform you fully own — unlike social media pages that can change their rules overnight.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

Absolutely. Our Business and Premium packages include a content management system (CMS) so you can update text, images, and blog posts without any coding knowledge. We also provide training and documentation to get you comfortable making changes on your own.

How long does it take to build a small business website?

Most small business websites are ready within 2 weeks of kickoff. More complex sites with custom features, ecommerce, or extensive content may take 3–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during the discovery phase so you know exactly when to expect your site to go live.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

Yes. We offer optional maintenance plans that cover security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support. However, there's no obligation — you own your website outright and can manage it yourself or bring in any developer you choose.
