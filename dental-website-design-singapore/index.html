The Challenge

Patients Are Choosing Their Dentist Online

Most patients in Singapore compare at least two or three dental clinics online before making an appointment. They look at treatment offerings, dentist credentials, before/after photos, and reviews — all within minutes. If your clinic's website is outdated, hard to navigate, or doesn't show up on Google, you're losing patients to competitors who invested in theirs.

Dental clinics face a unique set of challenges online. Patients want to see real results before committing to treatments like veneers, Invisalign, or implants. They want to book appointments without calling during office hours. And your website has to meet the Ministry of Health's advertising guidelines — no prohibited claims, no misleading language, and proper disclaimers where required.

Singapore's dental market is saturated with clinics competing for the same local patients. Whether you run a single practice in the heartlands or a multi-branch group across the island, your website is the first impression most patients will have of your clinic — and it needs to build trust from the very first click.

FAQ

Dental Website Design Questions Answered

How much does a dental clinic website cost in Singapore?

A professional dental clinic website in Singapore typically costs between $2,500 and $10,000+ depending on features. Our Starter package at $2,500 SGD includes a 5-page site with treatment pages, contact form, and Google Maps integration. Business and Premium packages add online appointment booking, before/after galleries, and multi-location support.

Do dental websites need to follow MOH advertising guidelines?

Yes. The Ministry of Health (MOH) and Singapore Dental Council have strict advertising guidelines for dental services. We ensure your website complies with these regulations — avoiding prohibited claims, using approved terminology, and including required disclaimers where necessary.

Can you integrate online appointment booking into my dental website?

Yes. Our Business package ($5,500) and above include online appointment booking integration. Patients can select a dentist, choose a treatment type, pick an available time slot, and receive automated confirmation — reducing phone calls and no-shows for your clinic.

Can you build a before/after gallery for dental treatments?

Yes. Before/after galleries are one of the most effective features for dental websites. We build filterable galleries organised by treatment type — such as teeth whitening, veneers, or Invisalign — with proper patient consent workflows and MOH-compliant presentation.

How long does it take to build a dental clinic website?

Most dental clinic websites go live within 2–3 weeks. A simple 5-page site can be ready in as little as 2 weeks, while larger projects with online booking, before/after galleries, or multi-location features may take 3–4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
